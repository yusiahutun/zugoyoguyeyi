Antoinette Biordi is an Emmy Award-winning reporter who has been in the news business since 1997. Antoinette began her career as a one-man band reporter, producing, shooting, writing and editing daily stories for News Center 7 in Ithaca, NY. Antoinette was raised most of her life on Long Island. Then in 1998 she worked as reporter/anchor for WETM (NBC) in Elmira, NY. In 1999 Antoinette worked at WNYT (NBC) in Albany, NY, and FOX 23 News in Albany. After 5 years in upstate New York, she jumped at the chance to be closer to home and began working as a freelance reporter at News 12 Long Island before working full time at News 12 Westchester in 2003.
