Cat Deeley looks phenomenal as she shows off toned legs in daringly short shorts

Publish date: 2024-05-09

So You Think You Can Dance presenter Cat Deeley looked absolutely flawless

    Cat Deeley never fails to impress with her daring looks and the star blew fans away on Tuesday as she posed on the beach alongside some friends in a striking look.

    The presenter showed off her phenomenal legs in the tiniest pair of frayed denim shorts as she allowed the sand to run through her feet. Her flawless outfit was completed with a shirt that featured a tropical print along a white cap that kept her beautiful blonde locks in check. Her friends looked equally as stylish in a pair of beach dresses, with one opting for a pair of sandals while the other joined Cat in going barefoot.

    Cat shared a thought that many would share with the hectic British weather, simply pining for warmer climes as she said: "Miss this," alongside a heart emoji.

    The mum-of-two is recently back from a trip to Costa Rica and she wowed with the string bikini that she wore during her time away.

    In a fun clip shared to her Instagram Stories, Cat revealed that an iguana had found its way into their villa as they enjoyed the sun, and she showed off her toned physique as she chased the lizard off.

    "My Lizard King," she wrote across the clip, before adding: "You're alright mate… you're alright."

    Cat, who is married to comedian Patrick Kielty, also shared a photo showing her and her group of friends walking on a beach at sunset. "Costa Rica… Lucky ladies", she wrote alongside tagging each one of them.

    Ahead of her trip to paradise, Cat enjoyed a family holiday with her sons, Milo, seven, and James, four. Cat is notorious for keeping them out of the spotlight but while away the star shared a sun-soaked photo with one of her little ones.
