Watermelon and Blue Cheese Salad Recipe

Publish date: 2024-02-23
Kana Okada

This summertime salad was created by Jean-Georges Vongerichten, owner of Jean Georges in New York City.

Read More

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Yields: 4

Prep Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 10 mins

Cal/Serv: 205

Ingredients

Save Recipe
  • 24 cube seedless watermelon
  • 3 oz. high-quality blue cheese
  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • Sea Salt
  • cracked white pepper

Directions

  • Step 1Divide watermelon cubes among 4 serving plates. Crumble blue cheese on top and drizzle with olive oil. Season generously with salt and pepper and serve.

    Nutritional information is based on a 3/4-cup serving
