Hourglass Dolphins are primarily found in the cold, deep waters of the Southern Ocean surrounding Antarctica.

Hourglass Dolphins are known for their impressive speed, capable of reaching speeds of up to 60 kilometers per hour (37 miles per hour).

Hourglass Dolphins predominantly feed on a diet of small fish and squid.
