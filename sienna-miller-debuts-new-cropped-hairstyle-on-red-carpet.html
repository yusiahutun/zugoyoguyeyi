Sienna Miller debuts cropped hairstyle as she supports fianc Tom Sturridge

Publish date: 2024-06-19

Known for her long, glossy locks, Sienna Miller was virtually unrecognisable as she unveiled a dramatic new look on Sunday night. Joining fiancé Tom Sturridge for the world premiere of his new film Effie Gray, Sienna stepped out with a short, cropped hairstyle, a stark contrast to her usually tousled waves.

Sienna Miller revealed her drastic new hairstyle on Sunday night

The 32-year-old fashionista ruled the red carpet in a bright-coloured, printed shift dress from Roksanda Ilincic's Resort 2015 collection, which she paired with black stilettos and a small handbag. To complement her straight, shorter locks, Sienna chose a ruby red coloured lipstick but kept the rest of her makeup to a minimum.

The British beauty has been dating Tom since 2011, and the couple have a young daughter called Marlowe.

Sienna and Tom have been dating since 2011

"I don't know what the new fashion trends are," admitted Sienna as fashion took a backseat after she gave birth to her daughter. "With my daughter, Marlowe, I don't have time to follow this anymore." 

Sienna and Tom made their debut as a couple for Burberry for the autumn/winter 2013 campaign, marking the first time they were professionally photographed together. "This was a shoot full of love and laughter," said Christopher Bailey, Burberry's chief creative officer. "It was such a treat to work with Sienna and Tom, and to capture their magic together."
