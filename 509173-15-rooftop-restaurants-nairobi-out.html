15 rooftop restaurants in Nairobi that you have to try out

Publish date: 2024-02-20

Nairobi, the bustling capital city of Kenya, offers a vibrant culinary scene that caters to every taste bud. Among the myriad dining options, rooftop restaurants in Nairobi stand out, combining delectable cuisine with breathtaking views. From the heart of the city's skyline, these elevated dining destinations provide an unparalleled dining experience.

If you are in Nairobi and looking for a unique experience, rooftop restaurants are the best. Their experience transcends the boundaries of traditional restaurant settings. Whether it's a romantic dinner under the starlit sky or a lively gathering with friends, rooftop restaurants in Nairobi promise an unforgettable experience.

Rooftop restaurants in Nairobi that you have to try out

Nairobi's rooftop restaurants cater to every palate and occasion. Indulge in the diverse flavours of Kenyan cuisine, international delicacies, and innovative fusion creations, all while being treated to sweeping vistas of Nairobi's dynamic skyline.

With their enchanting ambience and unparalleled vistas, these beautiful restaurants are a must-visit for any food enthusiast or adventurer. Here are some of the amazing rooftop restaurants in Nairobi that you can try out:

1. The Arbor

  • Location: 904 James Gichuru Road Lavington, Nairobi 00606 Kenya
  • Contact: +254 729 400291

The Arbor is a trendy rooftop eatery located in the heart of the city, with its chic decor and lush greenery. The Arbor offers a tranquil escape from the urban hustle, allowing guests to enjoy a delectable menu of fusion cuisine while overlooking Nairobi's skyline.

2. Level 8 Lounge and Bar

  • Location: 9042/1044 Tower Avenue, Nairobi Kenya
  • Contact: +254 20 2927000

If you are looking for an international experience in the heart of Nairobi, Level 8 Lounge is the place to be. Situated on the rooftop of the Mövenpick Hotel & Residences Nairobi, the Level 8 Lounge provides a tranquil oasis amidst the urban landscape.

Guests can enjoy panoramic views while sampling a delectable menu inspired by international cuisine.

3. Sarabi Rooftop Restaurant & Bar

Sarabi Rooftop Restaurant & Bar is one of the best rooftop restaurants in Westlands, Nairobi. It offers an exceptional experience that leaves a lasting impression on its clients.

4. Tin Roof Café

  • Location: Karen and Langata, Nairobi

  • Contact: +254 725 894477

6. Mercury Lounge

  • Location: ABC Plaza Lavington, Nairobi Kenya
  • Contact: +254 20 4451875

Mercury Lounge offers a rooftop escape in the heart of Westlands and a stunning view of Nairobi's skyline, particularly during sunset. With its vibrant and energetic atmosphere, this popular spot offers a diverse menu that blends African and Mediterranean flavours. Mercury Lounge is one of the best restaurants with a view in Nairobi.

7. The Nest Rooftop Lounge

  • Location: Tribe Hotel Limuru Road
  • Contact: 020 7200 000

Perched atop the luxurious Tribe Hotel, the Nest Lounge offers a breathtaking view of Nairobi's skyline. With its modern and chic setting, guests can indulge in an eclectic menu that showcases global flavours, complemented by creative cocktails.

8. The View Restaurant

  • Location: Movenpick Hotel, Westlands.
  • Contact: 0709 548 183.

The View Restaurant offers a truly unique dining experience that sets it apart from other rooftop restaurants in Nairobi. You may enjoy a stunning 360-degree view of the thriving metropolis of Nairobi from the revolving restaurant. Serving genuine Swedish, Swiss, and European cuisines.

9. Brew Bistro Fortis

  • Location: Woodvale Groove, Westlands Fortis Tower
  • Contacts: 0705 466836

Located in Westlands, Brew Bistro Rooftop is one of the rooftop restaurants near Nairobi CBD. It is a popular spot known for its lively atmosphere and craft beers. The rooftop area features a bar and outdoor seating, allowing visitors to enjoy the city views while indulging in delicious food and drinks.

10. Kilele Nyama

  • Location: Ibis Styles Hotel, Westlands.
  • Contact: +254 797 332473

Nairobi's most fashionable rooftop restaurant, Kilele Nyama, serves a variety of char-grilled chops. It features a breathtaking 360-degree panorama of the Nairobi skyline. The rooftop restaurant gives you a 5-star experience with exceptional customer service personnel.

11. Ki.chen Rooftop

  • Location: Senteu Plaza, 6th Floor, Nairobi
  • Contact: +254 701 950000

This is one of the best rooftop restaurants in Kilimani, with a heavenly view. The famous restaurant has a spectacular view with very affordable cuisine. In addition, it has an electrifying happy hour that ensures its customers are fully entertained.

12. Fifteen Rooftop

  • Location: 15th floor, the Curve by the Park Off Mombasa Road & Southern Bypass, Nairobi
  • Contact: 0111 151515

The Fifteen Rooftop is a trendy rooftop restaurant located in Nairobi. Situated on the fifteenth floor of a building, it offers a stunning panoramic view of the surrounding cityscape. This rooftop destination combines a chic and modern ambience with delectable cuisine to create a memorable dining experience.

13. Sky Lounge at Emara Ole-Sereni

  • Location: Emara Hotel Ole Sereni
  • Contact: 0732 191000

If you are looking for rooftop restaurants near Nairobi CBD, this is one of them. Perched atop the Emara Ole-Sereni Hotel, this elegant lounge provides breathtaking panoramic views of the Nairobi National Park, offering a unique juxtaposition of urban and natural landscapes.

The Sky Lounge at Emara Ole-Sereni promises an enchanting experience that combines exceptional cuisine with an awe-inspiring view of Nairobi's iconic wildlife and surroundings.

14. Hero Restaurant

  • Location: Village Market, 9th Floor, Trademark Hotel Village Market, Limuru Road, Limuru Rd, Nairobi
  • Contact: 0732 186666

Hero, a rooftop bar and restaurant in Gigiri, is situated on the 9th floor of the Trademark Hotel and offers a unique experience. The idea of the location is to honour local and comic book characters.

The Hero Restaurant is a distinctive and fascinating venue with many gorgeous and colourful murals on the walls, exciting superhero features, and fantastic views of the Nairobi skyline.

15. Tambourin

  • Location: Villa Rosa Kempinski in Westlands.
  • Contact: 0729 769253

Tambourin is a captivating rooftop restaurant located at the luxurious Villa Rosa Kempinski in Nairobi. Situated on the hotel's top floor, this Middle Eastern-inspired restaurant offers a truly enchanting dining experience.

Tambourin's menu features a delightful array of mezze, grills, and other traditional dishes that capture the essence of Lebanese and Moroccan cuisine. Alongside the delectable food, guests can indulge in handcrafted cocktails and a curated selection of wines.
