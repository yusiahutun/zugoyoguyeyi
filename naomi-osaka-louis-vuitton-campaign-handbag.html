Everyone wants the bag in Naomi Osakas new Louis Vuitton campaign

Publish date: 2024-08-26

The tennis star’s accessory isn’t available just yet, but we found one just like it in the meantime.

    Naomi Osaka has been leaving her mark in the fashion world in the past few months. The 23-year-old tennis star recently graced the cover of Vogue, with the fashion bible calling her “The People’s Champion.” She has also landed the cover of Japan and scored campaigns and collaborations with Strathberry and more. 

    The sporty fashionista took her campaign list to another level when she announced that she is Louis Vuitton’s new brand ambassador. “Wow this is actually crazy. Honored to be @louisvuitton newest House Ambassador. Funny story: I first bought my mom a LV bag when I was 16 as a birthday present and it’s kinda been a tradition since. Do I call this a full circle moment? Yes.”

    View post on Instagram 

    Naomi Osaka stuns in Louis Vuitton's SS21 Campaign

    In the photo, Naomi strikes a fierce pose with her naturally curly hair flowing around her, wearing a multi-colored Louis Vuitton dress and draping a signature crossbody bag over her arm from the brand’s Spring Summer 2021 collection.

There were none, so the fashion-obsessed Instagrammers flocked to Louis Vuitton’s Instagram page for answers. “What’s the name of the bag,” one wrote, along with many others. “NAME OF BAG!,” someone else added. “Love it! Can’t wait to get one,” another person chimed in.

That bag retails for $2,050 price tag, so we’re expecting the bag Naomi is holding to cost around the same.  We grabbed details on where to shop it below.
