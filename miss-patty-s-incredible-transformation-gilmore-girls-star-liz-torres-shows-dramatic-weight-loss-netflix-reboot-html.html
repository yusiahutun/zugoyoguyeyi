Gilmore Girls' Liz Torres shows off dramatic weight loss in Netflix reboot

Publish date: 2024-04-21

She starred as Gilmore Girls standout Miss Patty from 2000-2007.

But as the reboot was released on Netflix on Friday, Liz Torres' Miss Patty was almost unrecognizable.

The 69-year-old actress showed off a shocking weight loss, as she reprised her unforgettable role.

She looked svelte in a long, black dress and coordinating duster with gold embellishments, both of which fell to her ankles.

She also showed off long, dark tresses with grey streaks in the front that cascaded past her shoulders.

Back at it! Liz was almost unrecognizable in Gilmore Girls: A Year in the Life as Miss Patty, a role she had originated in the first run of the series, from 2000-2007

Svelte: The actress also cut off a much slimmer figure as she was spotted at the premiere of the Netflix reboot on November 18 (R), pictured (L) in 2003

Liz seemed to have begun losing weight around 2010, as she showed off a slimmer figure at the after-party for It Must Be Him's opening night in September of that year. 

And in addition to her svelte, new figure, Liz's Miss Patty also showed off a different hairstyle in the new Netflix reboot.

In the four-episode special she can be seen with long, sleek layered dark tresses in lieu of her signature, short, curled red hair.

A new look: In addition to the weight loss, Liz showed off a sleeker, darker 'do (R, earlier this month, L, in 2003)

Shedding pounds: Liz had first debuted a slimmer frame back in 2010 as she appeared at the after-party for the opening night of It Must Be Him

Following her time in the first run of Gilmore Girls, Liz had appearances on Private Practice, Desperate Housewives, Scandal, and Devious Maids.

Liz is next set to appear in upcoming films Chick Flick and Lost and Found En Cuba, due out sometime in 2017.
