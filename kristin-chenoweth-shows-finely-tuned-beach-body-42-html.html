She's a TV and Broadway star, but it seems all the world's a stage for Kristin Chenoweth.

The 42-year-old star turned heads as she strolled down the beach in a tropical blue and white swimming costume while holidaying on the Turks and Caicos Islands.

The singer looked every bit the beach babe as she relaxed in the sunshine with her tousled blonde hair and tanned skin.

Beach babe: Kristin Chenoweth Outside the Somerset Hotel, Turks & Caicos Islands in the West Indies

Cover up: The actress kept under wraps in a blue and white one piece

She was staying at the Somerset Hotel and Resort and spent time relaxing on the beach and swimming in the tropical water.

Last year Kristin talked to Women's Health magazine about how she keeps in shape.

'Three times a week my trainer, Mike Alexander, who got Jessica Simpson in shape, has me circuit training and doing cardio,' she revealed.

'I have boobs and a short torso, so I have to keep my core as thin as possible. But, even at my thinnest, I always have a little bit of a belly, so we work on that a lot.'

The 4ft 11in star is best known for her role as Sally brown in the Broadway production of You're A Good Man, Charlie Brown for which she won a Tony Award.

Play time: Kristin walked along the beach and played in the waves

She was also the first actress to play the role of Glinda, the good witch, in the musical Wicked and recently exercised her vocal talents with a guest-starring role on the hit television show Glee.

In the show she played April Rhodes, a former member of the glee club who never finished high school and ended up hitting rock bottom.

Her most popular television role is that of Annabeth Schott in NBC's The West Wing however in 2009 she won an Emmy Award for her portrayal of Olive Snook on ABC's Pushing Daisies.

She is returning to the big screen at the end of the year in a film entitled Family Weekend which started shooting in Michigan this month.

The film is about  a disgruntled teenager's attempts to get attention from her dysfunctional family.

When she doesn't receive what she wants the 16-year-old decides to take her parents hostage.

The film also stars Matthew Modine.
