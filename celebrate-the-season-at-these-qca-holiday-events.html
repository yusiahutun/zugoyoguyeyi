Celebrate the season at these QCA holiday events!

Publish date: 2024-02-09

The holidays are here, so check out these events across the QCA!

Tuesday, December 26

❄️ Winter Lights, 5:00-9:00 p.m., Quad City Botanical Center, 2525 4th Ave., Rock Island. The gardens will be in winter bloom with over 225,000 glowing lights during the seventh annual Winter Lights.

❄️ Fejervary Holiday Lights, 5:00-9:30 p.m., Fejervary Learning Center, 1800 W. 12th St., Davenport. Seasonal holiday lights display with new shows every 30 minutes. This is a park and watch event, not a drive or walk-through event.

❄️ Vander Veer Botanical Park Lights Display, Vander Veer Botanical Park, 215 W. Central Park Ave., Davenport. The annual holiday spectacular features a multitude of lights!
