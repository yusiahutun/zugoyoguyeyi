Biography

Jon Lester was born in Tacoma, WA on January 7, 1984. All-Star pitcher who began his career with the Boston Red Sox in 2006 and became the team’s ace shortly thereafter. He won the final game of the 2007 World Series for the Red Sox and helped lead the team to another World Series Championship in 2013. In 2016, he helped the Chicago Cubs win the franchise’s first World Series title since 1908. He was teammates with first baseman David Ortiz on the Red Sox from 2006 to 2014. He was a star pitcher in high school and was named the Gatorade Player of the Year in 2000.
