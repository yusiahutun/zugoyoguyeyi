Who is Rose Namajunas?

Rose was born on 29 June 1992, in Milwaukee, Wisconsin United States. This means she is 30 years old as of 2022. Rose Namajunas' parents are Jolita Frank (mother) and Arturas (father). Rose's mother was a pianist. She received her training at the Lithuanian Academy of Music and Theatre.

Her father died of pneumonia in 2008 in Germany when Rose was 16. He struggled with schizophrenia before his death. The mixed martial artist was raised alongside her brother Nojus Namajunas. What ethnicity is Rose Namajunas? Rose is of Lithuanian race and has an American nationality.

Educational background

She went to Milwaukee High School of the Arts for her high school education. She later proceeded to Minnesota Martial Arts Academy for her major in martial arts.

Career

The martial artist began training in Taekwondo at the age of five in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. At the age of 9, she earned her first belt, the poom belt (junior black belt). While in high school, she went on to practice karate, jiu-jitsu, and Kickboxing alongside other martial arts like Duke Roufus. In 2010, she started her career as an amateur in the MMA competition.

However, she made her professional debut on 5 January 2013 when she fought against Emily Kagan at Invicta FC 4: Esparza vs. Hyatt. Namajunas won the fight via submission due to a rear-naked choke in round three. She went on to battle Kathina Catron at Invicta FC 5: Penne vs. Waterson on 5 April 2013. She similarly won via submission due to a flying armbar just 12 seconds into the first round.

In July 2013, she faced off Tecia Torres at Invicta FC 6: Coenen vs. Cyborg and lost the fight by unanimous decision. She was later signed by the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) in December 2013 alongside other 10 other strawweight fighters. From there, she fought various popular contestants including Nina Amsaroff, Angela Hill, and Paige VanZant.

She, however, came to prominence in November 2017 when she defeated Joanna Jedrzejczyk and became the new UFC’s Women Strawweight Champion. She won the fight by KO in the first round. Aside from martial arts, Rose also has a black belt in Taekwondo and Karate. Additionally, she is a blue belt Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

Is Rose Namajunas Strawweight?

Yes, Rose joined the Strawweight championship in 2017 winning against Strawweight champion Joanna Jędrzejczyk. She later won her second reign as Strawweight champion when she faced Zhang Weili for the UFC Women's Strawweight Championship in 2021.

Awards and achievements

The American mixed martial artist has achieved various awards throughout her career. Some of the awards include:

  • Lithuanian American National Hall of Fame - Class of 2019
  • ESPY Award nominee (2018 for Best Fighter and 2021 for Best MMA Fighter)
  • Kids' Choice Sports 2018 Award for Biggest Powerhouse (nominee)
  • Eighth Annual Real Women Awards - 2017 Woman of the Year

Endorsements

Rose has also worked for various companies as a model. She has endorsed several companies such as Reebok, ONX Sports, and Monster Energy.
