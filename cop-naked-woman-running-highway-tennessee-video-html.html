Hilarious video shows cop faceplanting as he tries to tackle naked woman running around highway in T

Publish date: 2024-04-08

Startling video footage has captured the moment a police officer faceplanted as he attempted to tackle a naked woman running around a highway in Tennessee. 

The bizarre clip shows two cops approaching a nude, unidentified, tattooed woman standing near Highway 153 in Chattanooga.

As the policemen attempted to approach, the naked woman quickly sprinted away, leaving the officers straggling behind. 

One cop attempted to grab the woman, but missed, and ended up faceplanting on the ground, while the other continues the chase.

'Left the office for lunch just in time to see this!' Michael Porter wrote in the caption of the now-viral Facebook video. 'The things you see in Chattanooga! Cops vs Naked Lady!'

The videographer can be heard in the background narrating the chaotic scene unfolding. 

'This chick is naked man, oh my god!' he said. 'Only in Chattanooga.'

The video quickly circulated online, garnering over 200,000 views, with many people captivated by the outlandish nature of the events. 

While some found it hilarious, others noted the lack of physical endurance the cops displayed. 

'This is why Chattanooga's traffic is so bad,' one user joked in the comments. 

'I think it time the Chattanooga police put a little track time in,' another commented. 

'Stay classy, Tennessee. Stay classy,' another user sarcastically remarked. 

It's unclear at this time exactly when and where the incident took place - or why the woman was naked and running from police. 

DailyMail.com reached out to Chattanooga police for comment.
