Mandy Moores Velvet Dress and Suede Boots Combo Is Fully Fall

Publish date: 2024-04-14

Fall has officially been in swing for weeks now, but if you someplace where the chilly weather has yet to arrive, the season still might feel out of reach. But that's apparently not stopping Mandy Moore.

On Wednesday, the actress arrived on the set of the Today show in New York City, wearing a deeply autumnal outfit. For the appearance, Moore sported a sleeveless burnt orange velvet midi dress from Ulla Johnson’s pre-spring 2024 collection that featured gathered ruching at the waist and a thigh-high slit. She paired the vibrant fall frock with another cold-weather staple: a pair of brown suede knee-high boots. Dainty gold hoop earrings and a smattering of rings rounded out her look.

As for glam, Mandy styled her caramel-highlighted brunette hair in tousled waves with wispy curtain bangs, and added a swipe of pink lip gloss and a swirl of blush to her beauty look.

During her appearance on the talk show, Mandy opened up about raising her two sons — August "Gus" Harrison, 2, and 11-month-old Oscar "Ozzie" Bennett. “They’re the best,” she shared. “I’m one of three, so I just can’t imagine a world without siblings, and I always knew if I were lucky enough to have a family, I would love to have more than one, but I didn’t realize until the second came out, I think I could do this again. My husband thinks I'm crazy.”

As for a third, she added, “I have not closed that door."
