Meet Yung Miami's Son, Jai Malik Wiggins Jr.

Publish date: 2024-03-25

Jai Malik Wiggins Jr. is famous as the first child of popular City Girls rapper Yung Miami. Yung Miami, whose real name is Caresha Romeka Brownlee, is a popular American rapper who became famous for her song “Strub Tha Ground.”

Yung Miami is part of City Girls alongside JT. They got their big break after featuring on Drake’s single “In My Feelings.” Following this feature, the rap duo became a fan favorite in the music industry.

Despite being in the spotlight, Yung Miami has shielded her son from the media’s scrutiny, especially after the tragedy that struck the celebrity kid at a young age. Here’s everything you need to know about Yung Miami’s son.

Jai Malik Wiggins Jr.’s Parents Had a Rocky Relationship

Yung Miami’s son was born on June 26, 2013, to her then-boyfriend Jai Wiggins Sr. Although the famous City Girls rapper was only 19 at the time of his birth, she became an amazing mother. He is an American and is of African-American descent. 

His parents, Yung Miami and Jai Wiggins Sr., were in a relationship at birth. However, their relationship, which started in 2010, became rocky amid accusations of domestic violence, which saw the pair going their separate ways in 2015.

Shortly after their separation, Yung Miami filed a temporary restraining order in October 2015 following alleged physical harassment from her baby daddy. Jai Sr. reportedly attacked the rapper by punching her in the face after she approached him in his car to greet him. 

However, the pair resolved their issues to become loving and amicable co-parents to Jai Jr. Father, and son had a loving bond, and Yung Miami described Jai Jr. and Jai Sr. as “best friends.”

Jai Malik Wiggins Lost His Father At a Young Age

Jai Malik Wiggins Jr. suffered the loss of a father at such a young age. He lost his dad on June 15, 2020, after he suffered life-threatening injuries during a shoot-off. Even though his father was rushed into Intensive Care, he tragically died of the injuries. 

The exact circumstances surrounding his murder have not been made public yet. However, several speculations emerged regarding his untimely death. Although some sources claimed Jai Sr. died trying to defend his sister during a physical altercation that led to a shootout, these reports remain unconfirmed.

After his death, Yung Miami has come out several times to say how Jai Jr. misses his father. In a Twitter post, Yung Miami confessed that it saddened her to see how much her son misses Jai Sr., and it upset her that her son would grow up without his father. 

Meet Jai Malik Wiggins Jr.’s Sister

Although Jai Malik Wiggins Jr. is the only child his parents had together, he has a younger sister, Summer Miami Luellen. Summer is Yung Miami’s second child, which she had with her ex-boyfriend, Joshua Howard Luellen. Joshua is an American record producer famously known as Southside. He is popular for his work on the soundtrack of films like “Creed” and “SuperFly.”

Summer was born in October 2019. Her parents had an on-again-off-again relationship that started in 2018. However, Yung Miami and Southside broke up in 2020, ending their fluctuating relationship. Despite their separation, the ex-couple have a great relationship as co-parents to their adorable daughter.

Summer and her brother have a good relationship and flaunt each other on their media pages. Like her brother Jai Jr., Summer also has her own Instagram account, where her mum shares her photos. 

Yung Miami’s Son Is Active on Social Media

Like many celebrity kids, Jai Malik Wiggins Jr. is a budding social media sensation. The adorable kid has an Instagram account with over 75 thousand followers. He often shares cute photos of himself with his baby sister, Summer. 

In addition, he also has a YouTube channel with 1.23k subscribers. However, his YouTube page has only two videos, and they have over 14k views. Like most boys his age, he loves playing football, eating sushi, and watching movies with his sister.

What Is His Net Worth?

Yung Miami’s son doesn’t have a known career yet. Although Jai Malik Wiggins Jr. walked the runway for Nike at the New York Fashion Week in 2021, he relies on his mom as his primary caregiver. Hence, he doesn’t have a net worth. However, his famous mom has a net worth of over $2,000,000, which she earned from her career as a successful artist.

Like Nipsey Hussle’s son, Kross Ermias Asghedom, Jai Malik Wiggins Jr. has won the hearts of his mom’s fans. They are eager to see if he will pursue music like his mom or become a fashion model. However, nothing is set in stone for the youngster yet.
