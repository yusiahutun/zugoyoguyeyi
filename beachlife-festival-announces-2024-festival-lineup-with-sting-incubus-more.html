Beachlife Festival Announces 2024 Festival Lineup With Sting, Incubus &amp; More

Publish date: 2024-07-04

REDONDO BEACH (CelebrityAccess) – BeachLife, Southern California’s premier live music, art, and culinary event, is back again for its 5th year – taking over Redondo Beach’s picturesque waterfront May 3-5, 2024. Known for delivering a high-end experience, BeachLife has established itself as a must-attend event, featuring memorable performances from a diverse array of legendary top talent and emerging artists across various genres, including rock, indie, pop, jam, reggae, and more.

Allen Sanford, the Founder and promoter of BeachLife, has unveiled the exciting lineup for BeachLife 2024. Headlining sets will be presented by renowned artists such as Sting, Incubus, and My Morning Jacket. The lineup further includes DEVO, Dirty Heads, Trey Anastasio + Classic TAB, ZZ Top, Seal, Local Natives, Fleet Foxes, Santigold, and many more (full lineup listed below).

Since its inception in 2019, the festival has swiftly garnered recognition for encapsulating Southern California’s cultural essence into a distinctive and immersive experience. Beyond the stellar live musical performances, the event features a diverse culinary selection curated by acclaimed chefs, a unique on-stage dining experience, compelling art installations, and on-site activations spotlighting various brand-led interactive engagements. It goes beyond mere entertainment, incorporating fan involvement and advocating for philanthropic and sustainable initiatives, making it a comprehensive celebration of culture and creativity.

While music takes the spotlight at the BeachLife Festival, the distinctive DAOU SideStage dining experience is making a return with an exciting new twist this year. Recognized as the “best restaurant in the South Bay” by the Orange County Register, this 5-star dining opportunity is unparalleled, located on stage and curated by an award-winning team of chefs. Attendees can indulge in a four-course meal while enjoying performances by their favorite musicians, creating a truly unique “rockstar” dining experience. The lineup of chefs and details for booking table reservations will be announced shortly.

In addition to the exclusive DAOU SideStage restaurant, BeachLife Festival will feature a variety of food and beverage options. These include a substantial craft beer bar showcasing local LA and Southern California breweries, multiple bars, lounges, food vendors, and food trucks throughout the venue. Catering to diverse preferences, there will be vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free choices accessible to all festival-goers.

The festival grounds will not only resonate with music but also host art installations and on-site activations, offering brand-focused fan engagements, giveaways, photo opportunities, and sustainable initiatives. Additionally, attendees can explore boutique shopping, games, and interactive experiences, adding to the vibrant atmosphere of the event.

Philanthropy and community engagement hold a prominent role at BeachLife. Throughout the year and leading up to the annual festival event, BeachLife actively supports various organizations in Southern California’s South Bay community and beyond, particularly those dedicated to the preservation of beaches and oceans. In collaboration with the LA Kings Care Foundation and numerous national and local nonprofit organizations, BeachLife will organize a Silent Auction. The aim is to raise awareness and generate essential funding for these impactful causes.

For the year 2024, the philanthropic partners include Heal the Bay, Surfrider Foundation, Redondo Beach Education Fund, Wyland Foundation, and Redondo Beach Police Foundation. This collaborative effort underscores BeachLife’s commitment to making a positive impact on environmental and community initiatives, fostering a sense of responsibility and care for the local and global environment.

Tickets for the BeachLife Festival are currently available for purchase at www.BeachLifeFestival.com. General Admission (GA) tickets start at $159 for a single day and $399 for a 3-day pass. For those seeking flexibility, the General Admission Plus option allows for unlimited ins and outs. The festival is open to all ages, and children ages 6 and under can attend for free with a ticketed adult.

For a premium and exclusive experience, BeachLife offers the all-inclusive Captain’s Pass. This includes complimentary culinary and beverage offerings, an exclusive bungalow, elevated main stage views, parking, a commemorative gift bag filled with items from partners, and more.

Admiral Passes are limited and include 2-4 GA+ VIP or Captain tickets, along with a boat mooring for three nights. For added luxury, space, and privacy, a limited number of Private Cabana Suites are available for purchase. BeachLife also provides various ticket insurance options for purchase.
