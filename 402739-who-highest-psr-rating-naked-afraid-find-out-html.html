Who has the highest PSR rating on Naked and Afraid? Full list

Publish date: 2024-07-21

Primitivity Survival Rating (PSR) is the score a contestant is awarded for competing in a wilderness survival contest in the show dubbed Naked and Afraid. The show has attracted numerous contestants with winners in each episode emerging with different scores. So who has the highest PSR rating?

Naked and Afraid is a popular American Television series that airs on Discovery channel. Each episode of the series revolves around two people (a man and woman) called survivalists. They meet for the first time in the wilderness and are given the task of surviving there naked for twenty-one days. The survivalists are allowed to bring one helpful item, such as a fire starter or matchet.

Who has the highest PSR rating?

The contestants are ranked according to their Primitive Survival Rating (PSR). Naked and Afraid PSR scores are based on forecasts and observation of survival fitness in skill, experience, and mental strengths. The facilitators of the program measure the contestants' weights before and after the episode. Here are the scores of some of the contestants.

1. EJ “Skullcrusher” Snyder - PSR 9.1

EJ has three appearances on the popular television show. With 9.1, he has the highest PSR rating ever. Snyder is an extreme survivalist, adventurer, and retired army combat veteran. He is an expert in leadership, tracking, building shelter and equipment.

2. Laura Zerra - PSR 9.1

Laura is a survivalist and reality star. She gained wide fame from Naked and Afraid, for her appearance in the first season. She was born on September 16th, 1985, in Agawam, Massachusetts.

Zerra accepted the challenge along with EJ '"Skullcrusher" Snyder. They both had a PSR rating of 8.2 from their previous seasons and managed to score 9.1. She also boasts of the Naked and Afraid highest PSR.

3. Billy Berger - PSR 8.9

Billy is a primitive hunter, filmmaker, writer, and archaeologist. He is one of the highest-ranking survivalists, who embraces all things ancient and forgotten. He competed in the sixth episode of season one in Louisiana and scored an 8.9 score.
