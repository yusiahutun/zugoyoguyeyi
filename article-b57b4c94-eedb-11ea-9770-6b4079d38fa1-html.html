MDOT projects in Jones, Jasper, Forrest | News

Publish date: 2024-02-28

The Mississippi Department of Transportation has several projects in the works on highways in south Mississippi, including some in Jones, Jasper and Forrest counties.

“Each one of them is geared toward improving driver safety and increasing Mississippians’ quality of life,” said Tom King, commissioner of the Southern Transportation District.
