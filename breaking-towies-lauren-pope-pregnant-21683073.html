TOWIE's Lauren Pope pregnant with first baby with millionaire boyfriend Tony Keterman

Publish date: 2024-05-31

TOWIE star Lauren Pope has announced she is expecting her first child with long-term boyfriend Tony Keterman.

The 37-year-old reality star broke the news over Instagram, sharing a trio of polaroid pictures hinting at the pregnancy.

Captioned "pure happiness", Lauren made it clear the parents-to-be are chuffed to watch their family grow.

In one picture, Tony could be seen kissing her blossoming baby bump, while in another, she cradled her growing tummy.

A third polaroid showed the baby's ultrasound – though its gender has yet to be revealed.

As soon as the post was uploaded to her feed, Lauren's reality TV pals piled the comment section high with words of congratulations.
