Robert Pattinson's baby news traumatizes fans over Twilight flashback

Publish date: 2024-04-17

Robert Pattinson’s girlfriend Suki Waterhouse has seemingly confirmed that the pair are set to welcome their first baby together, and it has sent fans into meltdown.

Robert Pattinson, who is known for starring as Edward Cullen in the Twilight films, is set to become a dad for the first time. It comes after Suki Waterhouse, who he has been in a relationship with for about five years, appeared to announce the news on stage. And it is safe to say that his fans aren’t best pleased, with some reflecting on his character’s daughter in Twilight.

Suki Waterhouse announces that she is pregnant

While performing at Coronoa Capital Festival in Mexico yesterday (November 19), the movie star appeared on stage in a sparkling pink dress, sequined tights, and fringe-trimmed boots and addressed the crowd. She said: “I’m extra sparkly today because I thought it might distract you from something else going on.”

The actress, who recently appeared in Amazon Prime’s hit TV adaptation of Taylor Jenkin Reed’s novel Daisy Jones and the Six then opened her coat. This is where she revealed a growing baby bump to an excited crowd. She then joked: “I’m not sure if it’s working.”

Fans in meltdown over Robert Pattinson’s baby news

The celebrity’s fans were quick to react to the announcement, with some wishing that it was them in Suki’s shoes. Others joked about Edward and Bella Swan’s (played by Kristen Stewart) baby from the vampire-themed romance franchise being the celebrity couple’s baby’s namesake.

Acting can sometimes leave you feeling like a ‘failure’

Robert Pattinson has kept away from the limelight over the years. But he recently opened up over what it’s like to be a film star in the harsh world of Tinsel Town.

In a recent interview, the 37-year-old said being in Hollywood can lead to worries about unemployment and feeling like a “failure” even with a successful career like his.

He said: “I’m constantly thinking that you’re just going to spend the vast majority of your life unemployed and desperate and kind of feeling like you’re a total failure.

“I think that’s just what life is.”

The actor, who also played Cedric Diggory in Harry Potter and The Goblet of Fire said it’s extremely stressful.

“I feel like you’re specifically at a maximum most of the time you’re doing a job, and you’re employed for three months,” he added. “That’s the most stressful thing in the world.”
