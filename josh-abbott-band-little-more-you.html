Josh Abbott Band Need a 'Little More You' in Sweet New Love Song

Publish date: 2024-06-27

Josh Abbott Band have released their first taste of new music since the band's 2017 EP Until My Voice Goes Out with their new single, "Little More You."

"Little More You" features the organic instrumentation that has dominated much of Josh Abbott Band's work, including fiddle, banjo, touches of steel guitar and an emphasis on electric guitar. Those elements create a pleasant, down-home country sound that's paired with an electronic drum beat, juxtaposing traditional and modern production touches.

Frontman Josh Abbott does his best to convey the intended message of a feel-good country love song. His vocals are as warm as ever as he sings about needing more time with the woman he loves, and that's the strongest aspect of "Little More You." Relaxed and inviting, his vocals are enough to have you smiling along with the song.

The lyrics are a sweet ode to simply wanting more time with the one you love.

"I said this cup could use a little more whiskey / This porch could use a little more swing / This week could use a little more weekend / And we could use a little more Hank / This world could use a little more country / The sky could use a little more blue / Those bees could use a little more honey / And I could use a little more you," Abbott sings in the chorus.

Did You Know? "Little More You" is the lead single from Josh Abbott Band's upcoming EP, Catching Fire.
