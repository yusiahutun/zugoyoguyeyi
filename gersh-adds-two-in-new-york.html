Gersh Adds Two In New York

Publish date: 2024-08-19

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — The Gersh Agency is expanding their New York operation with the addition of two new agents at the company's Gotham offices.

According to Deadline Hollywood, Tovah Silbermann has joined Gersh as a personal appearances agent in the comedy department and Gabrielle Kachman joins the agency from Authentic Talent & Literary, where she worked as a manager.

Silbermann has experiencing in booking and producing live events, having founded the Empire Biscuit Comedy Festival in 2015. Joining Kachman at Gersh will be her clients Sarah Winter, Clare-Hope Ashitey, Natalie Paul, Alex Ferris and Rana Roy, Deadline said.

“Were excited to bring these two terrific young agents into the fold,” agency co-president Bob Gersh told Deadline. “We have no doubt they will be great additions to the New York office.” – Staff Writers
