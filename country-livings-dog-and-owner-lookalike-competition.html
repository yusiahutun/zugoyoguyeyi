Country Living Dog Lookalike competition at Burghley Horse Trials

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Country Living’s annual Dog & Owner Look-a-like competition took place at the CL Pavilion at Burghley Horse Trials last month – this hotly contested event involves dogs coming along with their owners in a bid to be judged the most similar twosome at the show.

This year, a boisterous pack of gorgeous four-legged entrants, accompanied by their equally gorgeous two-legged owners, went head-to-head. The competition judges – a representative from competition sponsor Harringtons, and Catherine Gee from Country Living – were faced with the task of choosing the dog and owner with the most striking resemblance.

After much deliberation (judges), barking (dogs) and general excitement (the crowd), the judges awarded top prize to worthy winners Henry and Cooper and their owner Jo Scully. Close runners-up were Pip and Kanga and their owners Tory and Susie.

Then just the dogs took centre stage for the Cutest Dog category – with over 20 entries it was an almost impossible task to choose between all the irresistible furry faces, but the judges were won over by the charms of Cavapoo, Ben, brought along by his owner David Russon. Runners up were Bluebell and Sydney and their owners James and Willa.

Our top winners both received an amazing prize of year’s supply of Harringtons dog food, and all the four-legged entrants went away with a rosette and a bag of dog treats. Thank you to our wonderful sponsor Harringtons, and to the dogs and owners who came along – we’ll be doing it all again at Burghley Horse Trials next year, so do come along and join the mayhem!
