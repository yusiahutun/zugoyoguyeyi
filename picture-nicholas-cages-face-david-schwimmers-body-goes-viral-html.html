Picture of Nicholas Cage's face on David Schwimmer's body goes viral

Publish date: 2024-05-23

Face-swapping reached an all-time high on the internet after a picture circulated of Nicolas Cage's face placed on David Schwimmer's body when he was playing Ross Geller in Friends.  

On Monday, a Reddit user called spaghetticondom911 shared the picture of Cage's face Photoshopped on the Friends actor's body, and people were shocked by how little it changed the facial features. 

'Ross from Friends with Nicolas Cage's face on him just makes him look more like Ross,' the Reddit user wrote when posting the picture. 

Shocked: On Monday, a Reddit user shared a picture of Nicolas Cage's face on David Schwimmer's body from when he played Ross Geller in Friends 

Similar? The picture made people think Cage looked even more like Ross than the actual picture of Ross. Pictured left is Cage and right is Schwimmer 

True: Commenters loved the post on Reddit and responded saying Cage looked exactly like Ross in the show 

With Cage's face on the body of Schwimmer playing Ross, it was hard to distinguish much of a difference between the two actors. 

Original: People joked that the face-swap picture looked more like Schwimmer as Ross Geller than the original 

The picture has since erupted on social media as people responded to the incredible similarities between the men. 

'Could he BE any more Ross,' one person hilariously commented on the Reddit post. 

Other people were in agreement with one person calling the picture 'mindbending' while another asked if 'we are sure that's not just Ross' face.'

But it appears there are some facial similarities between the two actors, especially from back in the 90s when Schwimmer played Ross on Friends. 

Although spaghetticondom911 posted the picture on Reddit, the similarities between Schwimmer and Cage were first unveiled on a YouTube channel entitled Derpfakes. 

The account dedicated a two-minute-long video to transforming Cage into multiple actor's and characters from films and TV shows. Schwimmer's face was just one swap included in the mix.

During the video, the creator also put the 55-year-old actor's face on Monica, Phoebe and Chandler from Friends. But the Ross switch was the face-swap that really stuck with fans. 

The Reddit post led to a number of people sharing the picture on Twitter for others to see how similar Cage's and Schwimmer's faces appeared. 

'I was today years old when I realized that replacing Ross from Friends’ face with Nicolas Cage’s face just makes him look even more like Ross from Friends,' one person wrote on a Twitter. 

Another person commented: 'How did I never know that Nicolas Cage played Ross in Friends?'
