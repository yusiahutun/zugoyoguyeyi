Elliott Museum Opening Preview Party

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Steven Swann, John Fedorek and Fernando Giachino

Supporters celebrated the Elliott Museum’s opening of two exciting new exhibitions: “Sight, Sound and Dynamics in the Sea: The Role of Technology in Ocean Exploration,” by FAU/Harbor Branch, that features technology used to study the deep sea and local waterways. Sharing the gallery space is the Smithsonian exhibition “H2O Today,” which reviews the importance of water in our lives.
