Agnes Tirop: Slain Athlete's Family Says She Lived in Fear of Her Lover, Was Lured To His House

Publish date: 2024-08-11
  • Agnes Tirop was found dead on Wednesday, October 13, with stab wounds on her neck and abdomen
  • The family of the 25-year-old is still reeling in shock following her untimely and gruesome death
  • They said the athlete's lover was not her husband adding she lived in fear of her life

The family of slain athlete Agnes Jebet Tirop has revealed how their daughter lived in fear of her lover, Ibrahim Kipchumba Rotich who is believed to be behind her gruesome death.

The celebrated female long-distance runner was found dead on Wednesday, October 13, with stab wounds on her neck and abdomen.

Speaking to TV47, her inconsolable mother recalled the last time she saw her daughter alive on Sunday, October 10, only to learn of her death a few days later.

Her worst fear was confirmed when she found her unreachable on her phone and knew something bad had happened.

"I feel so much pain because she was always there for me," she said struggling to contain her tears.

Lived in fear

According to the deceased's brother Josephat Keter, the prime suspect was not quite her husband as widely reported.

Keter said his sister lived in fear of Rotich and had moved from the house they lived together to a camp due to fears over her life.

"She was lured (to the house) after she arrived in the camp. We think there is something that guy wanted and maybe he lied to her that he wants to return her properties and that was why he went to that house.She was no longer staying in that house. She was living in the camp because she really feared the man," he said.

Confesses to murder

TUKO.co.ke earlier reported that Ibrahim Kipchumba Rotich called her family and confessed to killing the world record holder.

According to the Keiyo North Police Commander Tom Makori, Tirop's father, on the evening of Tuesday, October 12, filed a report that the athlete was missing.

On Wednesday, October 13, at around 9am, Makori said Tirop's husband called his parents informing them that he had committed the heinous crime and sought God's forgiveness.

After the call, the 25-year-old's family, friends and neighbours rushed to her house and after breaking in, found her body lying in bed in a pool of blood.
