John Stamos Wedding Message to Mary-Kate Olsen

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Update, 12/1: Over the weekend, Mary-Kate Olsen married her boyfriend of 5 years, Olivier Sarkozy in a quiet NYC ceremony. Last night, John Stamos took to Instagram to congratulate his former Full House co-star with the sweetest throwback Instagram picture. Stamos captioned the photo of he and the young Olsen twins, "In the blink of an eye." Congrats MK. X" Guess there was no bad blood over the Olsens not returning for the Fuller House sequel after all. 

Original Article, 11/29: The Olsens are known for keeping their private lives private, and Mary-Kate's wedding was no exception. Bucking rumors of a summer wedding in the Hamptons, the former actress turned designer wed her boyfriend of three years, French banker Olivier Sarkozy, in a hush-hush ceremony on Friday.

Page Six reports the reception took place at a private residence in Midtown East Manhattan. Cocktails were held in a backyard garden before dinner. As expected, guests were asked to hand in their phones and devices beforehand to ensure the privacy of the couple—so pictures from the wedding have yet to be seen. Needless to say, the biggest question we're left wondering is: What did the bride wear??
