She recently renewed her wedding vows to her husband of 30 years, Ken Todd.

And now the couple are celebrating with a second honeymoon in Bora Bora.

And the 52-year-old Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills star certainly gave Todd a treat.

Red hot: Real Housewife Of Beverly Hills star Lisa Vanderpump, 52, slipped into a revealing scarlet bikini to celebrate her 30th wedding anniversary with husband Ken Todd in Bora Bora

Looking fabulous in her fifties, the reality star slipped into a red-hot two-piece bikini. 

With its low-cut top and high-cut polka dot bikini bottoms, the scarlet swimsuit accentuated the British restaurateur's sensational curves.

Lisa paired the sexy suit with some sky-high nude platforms and mirrored Aviators.

Meanwhile, Todd opted for a pair of black swimming trunks with the word 'Italia' emblazoned up the leg.

The couple posed for a happy snap in front of the picturesque Tahitian beach.

The image looked postcard worthy with a lone palm tree situated on a tiny island and crystal blue waters in the background.

Beaming for the camera, the pair looked relaxed and extremely happy to spend some quality alone time together.

Reality couple: Lisa and Ken appear on the hit reality show Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills

Lisa and Todd renewed their wedding vows in a secret ceremony earlier this month ahead of their 30th anniversary, and reportedly filmed the action for upcoming final scenes of season three of their  reality show.

The intimate event was held at the couple's Los Angeles mansion, according to Wet Paint Entertainment.

Apparently aware that virginal white is no longer for her, Lisa opted for a black evening gown, while Ken sported a pink shirt under his black jacket.
