Maidenhead United FC: A Rich History

Maidenhead United FC is a professional football club located in Maidenhead, Berkshire. It was founded in 1870 and has since become one of the oldest football clubs in England. The team currently competes in the National League, the fifth tier of English football.

Home Ground: York Road Stadium

The proud home of Maidenhead United FC is the historic York Road Stadium. Established in 1871, it holds the distinction of being the oldest continuously used football ground in the world. The charming stadium has a seating capacity of 4,000 and provides a fantastic atmosphere for supporters.

Maidenhead United FC’s Rivalries

Maidenhead United FC has a long-standing rivalry with Slough Town FC, known as the “Berks & Bucks derby.” The matches between the two teams are highly competitive and intensify the local football fervor. The rivalry adds an extra layer of excitement to the football scene in the area.
