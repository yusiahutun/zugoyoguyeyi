Bikini-clad Olivia Rodrigo 'slays' exotic vacay eating fried food poolside

Publish date: 2024-04-15

Singer and actress Olivia Rodrigo is enjoying some time out in an exotic location in 2023. The 20-year-old shared some snaps of her latest holiday in an Instagram photo slider captioned: “vacay dumpppp,” on July 26. When Olivia isn’t creating hit songs such as vampire, or appearing in High School Musical: The Musical, it seems that she’s living life to the fullest on vacation.

Olivia began singing at four years old and landed her first acting role at 12. Taking to Twitter in 2022, the singer wished a Happy Birthday to a song that “changed her whole life.” Now, she’s set to release her second studio album, GUTS, on September 8. The star graced the cover of Vogue magazine, so 2023 looks to be Olivia’s year.

Olivia ‘slays’ exotic vacay in white bikini

Taking to the ‘gram on Wednesday, July 26, Olivia Rodrigo shared that she was taking a vacation in Hawaii.

The singer and actress tagged her post at the Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea.

Her collection of snaps shows her enjoying the magnificent scenery of Maui including waterfalls and cute-looking wildlife.

Many of Olivia’s fans took to the comments section of her post to write that she is “slaying,” in her white bikini.

Star enjoys fries and chicken tenders

Sitting poolside in one of her photos, Olivia is seen enjoying some fries and chicken tenders on vacation.

Many of her fans and followers wrote on her post that they wanted to hit a “respect button” for the star.

Some commented on the deja vu singer’s post: “chicken tenders at the pool is such a slay,” and more said: “chicken tenders for the win.”

Madison Hu joins Olivia on vacation

In her ‘vacay dump’ of snaps, Olivia included some photographs of her Bizaardvark co-star Madison Hu.

Madison and Olivia are clearly great friends, as they spend time in Hawaii together.

The former Disney Channel stars are often pictured together on one another’s Instagram pages.

In 2021, Madison took to Instagram to wish her best friend a Happy Birthday and captioned her post: “happy birthday liv love u forever!!!”
