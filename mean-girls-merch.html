Cutest 'MeanGirls' Merch Inspired by the Musical Movie

Publish date: 2024-02-25

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

I’m old enough to have seen the OG Mean Girls and I’ve already seen the new musical film so I can confidently say, it feels like 2004 all over again. Fetch is back. Pink cardis are back. And the Mean Girls cast is on fire. It’s making me crave cute Mean Girls merch all over again and luckily, there are some fun products to help you get into the spirit.

Now, there’s not nearly as many Mean Girls collabs as there were for Barbie. Maybe that’s because Mean Girls-inspired clothes and accessories have been around since the early 2000s. But that doesn’t mean we don’t want new and fresh ideas — especially since the film is based off Mean Girls the musical more than the movie. Where’s our “Modern Feminism” tee?

Interestingly, when it comes to Mean Girls, there are a ton of food and drink collabs. Maybe that’s because the teens are, well, teens, and spend time in the cafeteria. There’s apparently a pink, off-the-menu Frappuccino (a White Chocolate Crème Frappuccino with strawberry puree and sweet cream) and even Cinnabon pink cinnamon rolls. Grab your drink, a snack, and shop the below fetch items.

  • American Eagle Tee

    Leave it to American Eagle to roll out a cute Mean Girls-inspired line with 2000s-era denim mini-skirts and baby tees.

  • ELF Makeup

    Did you catch E.L.F.’s cameo in the new movie? To celebrate, the affordable fave created an Every Day We Wear Pink Bundle featuring Jelly Pop Dew Primer (back for a limited time!), Camo Liquid Blush, Liquid Metallic Eyeshadow, Cream Glide Lip Liner, and Glow Reviver Lip Oil.

  • Coffee mate Liquid Creamer 

    OK so Coffee mate’s limited-edition Pink Creamer is a little random but also a lot of fun. The non-dairy creamer has a “sweet frosting flavor” and adds a pop of pink to your drink.

  • Olympia Le-Tan Clutch

    For the ultimate Mean Girls fan, pre-order iconic handbag designer Olympia Le-Tan’s Mean Girls The Burn Book Clutch Bag. It’s made from embroidered cotton, wool, and brass, and comes with a removable shoulder strap.

  • Mean Girls Sweatshirt

    The cozy Mean Girls A Little Bit Dramatic Long Sleeve White Adult Hooded Sweatshirt is inspired by Regina’s iconic tank top.

  • Mean Girls Mug

    A Mean Girls-inspired mug for all your pink drinks.

  • PacSun Shirt

    My favorite, iconic, perfect Mean Girls quote on a cozy cotton tee.

  • Mean Girls Faux Pocket Shirt

    We all could use a little Damian with us throughout the day.

  • Profusion Makeup

    For a more literal makeup collection, Profusion Cosmetics has an entire Mean Girls line featuring eyeshadow palettes, faux lashes, and brushes.
