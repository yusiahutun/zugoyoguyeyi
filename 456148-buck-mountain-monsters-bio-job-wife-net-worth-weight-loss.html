Rise to fame

Lowe struggled a bit when he joined the AIMS team during his first season of the Mountain Monsters show. He had trouble keeping up with the rest of the hunters and had a few fumbles with his gun. John Tice, the AIMS team leader, also revealed that he was sceptical initially. As a result, he was given the nickname "rookie" by the rest of the team.

Despite being a rookie in hunting mythical creatures, Jacob was always willing to do the grunt work. Over time, he also became an experienced hunter and could easily keep up with the rest of the team. On top of that, he is always the first one to jump into any dangerous situation when they are hunting monsters.

As a result, he graduated from his rookie status and was given the nickname "Buck" – a fitting name for his big frame and his headstrong and courageous personality. Jacob has been a part of the Mountain Monsters show since 2013. He has appeared in all of the 80 episodes of the reality TV program and is expected to return in the show's ninth season.
