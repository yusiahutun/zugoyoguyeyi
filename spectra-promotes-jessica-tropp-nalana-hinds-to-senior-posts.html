Spectra Promotes Jessica Tropp &amp; Nalana Hinds To Senior Posts

Publish date: 2024-08-09

PHILADELPHIA, PA (CelebrityAccess) — Venue management company Spectra announced the promotion of Jessica Tropp and Nalana Hinds to senior roles in their national leadership team.

Tropp, who is a twelve-year Spectra veteran was named as Senior Vice President, Business Development, Sales Support and Production at the company.

She will be tasked with oversight of business proposals, RFP responses, and corporate marketing materials for Venue Management, Food Services & Hospitality, and Partnerships.

Spectra also promoted Nalana Hinds to Senior Vice President, Corporate Controller. Hinds joined Spectra in 2018 as Vice President of Corporate Controller and since then, she’s been tasked with building out the Food Services & Hospitality accounting group as well as overseeing various treasury and tax issues.

Additionally, Hinds serves as a mentor to Spectra’s corporate accounting team across the Venue Management, Food Services, and Partnerships divisions.
