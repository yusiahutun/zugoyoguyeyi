Celebrity Birthdays 2-6 July: Margot Robbie, Sylvester Stallone and Tom Cruise

Publish date: 2024-10-07

2 July: Margot Robbie turns 28

Happy Birthday to actress Margot Robbie, who turns 28 on Monday! After the phenomenal success of I, Tonya, the Oscar-nominated film she produced with her husband Tom Ackerley and starred in, Margot has yet more to celebrate as she turns another year older. On Saturday, it was revealed that Margot will co-produce a Shakespearean drama for American network ABC. Whilst she is keeping mum about the topic at the moment, if her past success is anything to go by, we're willing to bet that Margot's next project will be a big hit too!

2 July: Lindsay Lohan turns 32

Sharing her birthday with fellow actress Margot is Mean Girls star Lindsay Lohan, who turns 32. The actress is spending the special occasion at the Lohan Beach House, the resort she owns on the idyllic Greek island of Mykonos. Taking to Instagram to document her big day, Lindsay revealed the special birthday surprise she received. "The best gift on my birthday is my sister surprising me in Mykonos," wrote Lindsay alongside a sweet family video from her sun-drenched trip. Lindsay's sister Aliana wrote a loving birthday message via Insta Stories: "Happy birthday my beautiful angel sister. I am so blessed to have you as my sister," she said of her older sibling.

3 July: Tom Cruise turns 56

Happy 56th Birthday to Hollywood actor Tom Cruise! The star turns 56 on Tuesday and proves he is as agile as ever in new behind the scenes footage he recently shared with his 1.7 million Instagram followers. Tom, who famously performs all his own stunts, can be seen leaping across 70-metre drops whilst filming for Mission: Impossible Fallout, the latest instalment of the franchise he stars in. 

4 July: Prince Michael of Kent turns 76

Happy Birthday to His Royal Highness Prince Michael of Kent, cousin of the Queen, who turns 76 on Wednesday. The royal is bound to spend his special day surrounded by his large family. He shares son Lord Frederick Windsor and daughter Lady Gabriella Windsor with his wife of 40 years, Princess Michael of Kent. The couple also have two grandchildren, Maud and Isabella, who are the daughters of Lord and Lady Frederick Windsor.

6 July: Sylvester Stallone turns 72

Happy Birthday to Rocky legend Sylvester Stallone, who celebrates his 72nd Birthday on Friday! The veteran actor could be having joint celebrations with one of his three daughters, Sistine, who turned 20 last week. Sylvester took to Instagram to share a picture of Sistine with her "very very very bizarre birthday cake" made by her mother, model Jennifer Flavin. We wonder if Sylvester's birthday cake will look as interesting…
