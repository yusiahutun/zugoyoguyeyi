Career

A singer-songwriter from the United States Kelley has four hit singles on the Billboard Adult Top 40 chart, having recorded for Hollywood Records, Threshold Records, and DNK Records as a pop-rock artist. His songs “Amazing” and “Just You” both charted in the top ten.

Kelley signed with MCA Nashville in 2010 and started a career in country music. “Georgia Clay,” his first country single, reached the Top 20 on the Hot Country Songs charts. Kelley is married to Katherine Heigl, an actress, and model.
