Fashion Fix: Everything you need to buy from Victoria Beckham's brand

Publish date: 2024-08-09

We take a look inside the celebrity-adored label ahead of her 50th birthday

    Victoria Beckham launched her inaugural Ready-to-Wear line in 2008 at New York Fashion Week, unveiling a curated set of 10 dresses that prioritize craftsmanship, silhouette, and fit. 

    She showcased the collection herself to an exclusive circle of fashion journalists and buyers at the Waldorf hotel, detailing the meticulous design and structure of each piece. Each dress was sequentially numbered—a tradition that continues—and the collection was released in limited editions at nine prestigious stores worldwide. She quickly won over the once-apprehensive fashion set. "I can’t quite believe I’m writing this, It was a very impressive, accomplished collection, with not a single dud," wrote Lisa Armstrong, "True there were only 10 designs, in various colours, each adhering to the aesthetic the designer has favoured in her own wardrobe of late: slender calf-length fitted dresses with raised waists. But it was the fabrics (silk, wool and organza) and the attention to detail that impressed."

    Her eponymous label is famed for its smart tailoring and flattering draping, but these styles aren't all that the celebrity-loved label is known for. The British brand is also brilliant at creating stylish eyewear and structured coats. The brand self-identifies as "the modern woman’s true wardrobe", priding itself on creating pieces that are "versatile and wearable yet rooted in a distinctly luxury sensibility that ignites the imagination". Even the royal household has recognised Victoria Beckham's work, in 2017 she was awarded an Order of the British Empire for her services to the fashion industry.

    Here's everything you need to know about Victoria Beckham's fashion brand...

    What brand does Victoria Beckham own?

    Unsurprisingly, the brand was set up by Victoria Beckham herself back in 2008. She first launched a womenswear line consisting of figure-hugging dresses featuring statement zips, in organzas, silks and taffetas. Some critics were initially doubtful, but the former Spice Girl has impressed some major industry names over the years.

    Where is the Victoria Beckham store?

    In 2014, a year after debuting her e-commerce site, Victoria Beckham expanded her fashion empire by opening her flagship store at 36 Dover Street in London’s chic Mayfair district. The space, a three-story testament to modern elegance, was designed by the celebrated London architect Farshid Moussavi. It encapsulates the designer's ethos of refined materials, skilled craftsmanship, and contemporary design in a boldly sophisticated setting. This versatile interior was crafted to host a dynamic array of installations and events (she even hosted her office Christmas party there), enhancing the brand's immersive retail experience.

    Which celebrities wear Victoria Beckham?

    Some famous fans of the label include Eva Longoria and Meghan Markle – both close friends of the fashion designer – but they're by no means alone in their appreciation for the cult label. Other celebrities who have worn pieces by Victoria Beckham include Beyoncé, Kim Kardashian and Kate Winslet.Plus, who could forget the recent wedding of Victoria and David Beckham's eldest son Brooklyn to heiress Nicola Peltz? The mother-of-the-groom wore a silver satin dress by her own label, and also dressed various members of the wedding party including model Mia Regan, the girlfriend of her son Romeo, and journalist Zanna Roberts Rassi.

    Where is the Victoria Beckham fashion show?

    In 2018, to celebrate the decade since the launch of her label, Victoria Beckham moved her runway show from New York to London Fashion Week. She then said 'cheerio' to London and moved the collections to show at the catwalks of Paris Fashion Week in 2022.

    Who designs Victoria Beckham clothes?

    Lara Barrio stepped into the spotlight as the new design director at Victoria Beckham, with buzz building around her debut collection in pre-spring 2023. Barrio, a seasoned power player in the fashion scene, held the reins as the design head at Ferragamo and boasts significant stints at major labels like Acne and Loewe. Her move to Victoria Beckham signalled at the evolution of this iconic brand.

    How many Victoria Beckham stores are there?

    Victoria Beckham's  fashion footprint extends to 230 retail locations across 50 countries, available online at VictoriaBeckham.com and featured in personalised boutique spaces within premier department stores.
