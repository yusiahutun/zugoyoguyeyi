Best Grilled Apples with Prosciutto and Honey Recipe

Publish date: 2024-04-16
Hector M. Sanchez

Read More

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Yields: 6 - 8 serving(s)

Prep Time: 30 mins

Total Time: 30 mins

Ingredients

Save Recipe

  • Canola oil, for grill grates

  • 1

    large Honeycrisp apple, cored and cut into twenty-four 1/8-inch slices

  • 2 tbsp.

    olive oil

  • Kosher salt and ground black pepper

  • 3 oz.

    thinly sliced prosciutto

  • 1

    (3-ounce) log goat cheese, crumbled

  • 1/4 c.

    chopped salted pistachios

  • 4 tsp.

    chopped fresh thyme

  • Pure honey, for drizzling

Directions

  • Step 1Heat grill to medium heat. Once hot, clean and oil grill grates with canola oil. 
  • Step 2Brush apple slices with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper. Wrap with prosciutto, dividing evenly. 
  • Step 3Grill over direct heat, uncovered and turning once, just until grill marks appear, 3 to 4 minutes. Transfer to a platter and top with goat cheese, pistachios, and thyme. Drizzle with honey.
