20 powerful prayer points for a crusade to be successful

Publish date: 2024-09-01

A crusade cannot achieve its purpose without delivering powerful prayers by the minister and his congregants. Through such an event, churches seek to recruit new members from the crowds attracted by the colourful crusades. While many people attend for the music played, having a minister who can deliver moving sermons and prayers helps people make up their minds. This cannot be achieved without relying on the best prayer points for a crusade.

Prayer is one of the most effective ways to communicate with God and has for his mercy and favour. When praying, you do not ask for things from God aimlessly. Instead, one has to identify the things to pray for, where prayer points for a crusade come in.

Prayer points for a crusade

You can’t keep praying for just one thing when there are so many things you can pray for. Prayer points can vary based on the unique characteristics of the audience. Below are prayer points for a successful crusade:

1. Crusade opening prayer

The best prayer points in preparation for a crusade program acknowledge God's presence from the onset. An opening prayer serves to ask God to take charge of all the crusade activities from the beginning to the end to ensure its success.

2. Crusade closing prayer

Everything with a beginning must have an end, and so is the case for a crusade. Since the crusade started with a word of prayer, it is to also end with one. Thank God for his guidance and protection throughout the crusade, and ask him to lead everyone home safely.

3. Ask God for boldness to witness

Miracles happen every day, but without God blessing us with boldness to witness, we may not be able to witness them. These are the prayer points for an upcoming crusade to help members to see the good work of the Lord. By witnessing, Christians will see the good deeds and spread them far and wide.

4. Thank God for the privilege of your calling

The truth is that not all people show interest in crusades even when they are in their vicinity. Therefore, this prayer point is for the minister to thank God for being called to lead his flock. It is also thanks to the almighty for making members of the crusade heed the call to attend.

5. Pray to break loose from every inherited evil curse

Many of those attending crusades are people seeking answers from God as to why their lives are filled with misfortunes even as they are prayerful. To answer them, you have to pray for them to break loose from every inherited evil curse derailing their progress. Once they have been set free, they will be able to realize all of God's goodness.

6. Thank God for crusade members

A crusade is not a crusade if people do not attend. For a crusade to be successful, it must attract many congregants hungry for the word of God. The work of God is evident in the big crowds he sends to crusades to witness his word hence he should be praised for that.

7. Ask God for opportunities

People want to see the results of the blessings spoken unto them at the crusades. God is kind and gives to those who ask. Therefore, a crusade is a perfect place to ask the Almighty to provide opportunities for all those in the crusade. Through those opportunities, they will give back to God to thank him.

8. Ask God to give those present faith to believe

People will hear the word of the Lord, but without faith, they will not believe it. Therefore, pray to God for the gift of faith so that those present will listen and believe His word. In the same way, let their faith be seen through good deeds because the Bible says faith without action is dead.

9. Ask God to bless the families of crusade members

All people want their families to grow in number and wealth. A crusade, therefore, dedicates a prayer to God to bless not only those present but also all their family members. Rebuke the spirit of sickness from their families so that they may have good health and long lives.

10. Thank God for providing deliverance from bondage

The chains of bondage have enslaved many people who are forced to serve Satan instead of God. Those that have mustered the courage to come to the crusade have God to thank because he is the one who has delivered them from bondage. Therefore, prayer should be said to thank Him and ask Him to continue protecting all his people.

11. Seek God’s mercy and forgiveness

No matter how perfect we think we are, we will always be imperfect in the eyes of the Lord. Therefore, we should use every opportunity to seek his mercy for our shortcomings. This prayer is important because it is through God's mercy and forgiveness that we will be able to avoid the fire of hell.

12. Call on God to cover crusade members with the blood of Jesus

Sin and evil are always knocking, and it is easy to find yourself giving in to them. Only the blood of Jesus can keep you safe by keeping evil from you. Since Christ shed his blood on the cross for us to be saved, we shouldn't have to suffer again, which is why we make this prayer.

13. A prayer for cleansing

While God and others may forgive us, it is more difficult to forgive ourselves. Therefore, prayer for cleansing is to help all crusade members get rid of their guilt and burdens. In a crusade, such a prayer can be done by incorporating symbolic actions like washing feet or faces to signify the cleansing of individuals.

14. Pray for peace

There are millions of people worldwide who do not know what peace is because of ongoing wars. Make this a prayer point for a crusade to pray for such people to have a taste of peace in their lives. It is also an opportunity to thank the highest for the relative peace and calm in your life.

15. Ask God to bless crusade members with giving hearts

Even in bountiful times, some people are constantly struggling to get by. Giving is a powerful prayer point for a crusade as it encourages participants to develop a compassionate and unselfish spirit.

This will motivate them to give freely to the less fortunate and the church without condition. Crusade members can also be encouraged to pray for a specific needy cause to help them connect on a deeper level.

16. Prayer for little children to grow knowing God

Little children occupy a special place in the kingdom of God hence they should also be encouraged to attend crusades. Use the opportunity to pray for them so that they may remain with the humility they were born with as they grow. Pray for their faith to grow so that they may worship none other than God.

17. Prayer for the spirit of community

All churches design their worship to take advantage of the spirit of the community. A crusade is not done for individuals but all members of the community. Therefore, it is the perfect place to encourage those present to appreciate and participate in their community.

18. Pray for truth

Truth is the only thing that can set the soul free, and believers should always be encouraged to be truthful no matter what. Without prayer, people tend to have trouble accessing the truth in their souls and letting it speak freely. Invite those at the crusade to meditate and pray to address, accept and speak the truth.

19. Pray for mothers

The church places a special emphasis on the role of mothers in raising children, following the example of Mary. She was a prayerful woman hence God chose her to be the mother of Jesus hence other women should be encouraged to have a similar relationship with God. This can work well as one of the prayer points for an outreach program targeting women.

20. Pray for the gift of love

It is the call of Christians to try and incorporate the love of God into all their affairs since Christ taught that love is the greatest of all gifts. The problem is that it is never easy for humankind to practice such immense love. Nevertheless, the many Bible readings and psalms that address love can be used as prayer points for a crusade.
