Cushionaire Cork Footbed Sandal

Two-strap sandals are the summer staple I’m now convinced I need for spring thanks to Katie Holmes. Fortunately, this trending pair from Cushionaire is on sale for just $30. This sandal is made with two wide, belted straps and a cork footbed that shoppers say feels like “walking on a supportive cloud.” “I have problems with my legs and feet aching all the time [and] the relief I've received from wearing these sandals has been unreal,” said another fan. 

Prettygarden Eyelet Top

This top from Prettygarden might rank lower on  the Movers and Shakers fashion list, but it’s topping my wishlist. The blouse is darling with an eyelet design, puffed short sleeves, and loop-button closures. It’s a combination that makes the $45 top “look much more expensive than it is,” according to shoppers, with one person even comparing it to a $150 lookalike, explaining that the “quality and details are great.” And while I’m really loving the forest green shade, it’s available in 18 other colors.

Innersy 6-Pack Hipster Underwear

This best-selling pack of hipster-cut underwear is somehow moving even higher up the Movers and Shakers ranking, now boasting more than 32,100 Amazon fans. “[They’re] absolutely the most comfortable panties I’ve ever worn,” raved one shopper, while another explained that the underwear’s cotton and spandex blend keeps them in place while being “so comfortable” you “forget you have them on.” They’re the “perfect everyday undies,” the same person wrote, adding that the style doesn't ride up, shrink, or fade. Okay, adding them to my cart immediately.
