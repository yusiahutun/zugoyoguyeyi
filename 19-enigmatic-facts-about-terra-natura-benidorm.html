FAQs

1. What are the opening hours of Terra Natura Benidorm?

Terra Natura Benidorm is open daily from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

2. Are there any age restrictions for the water park?

Yes, children under 130cm in height must be accompanied by an adult in the water park area.

3. Is food available inside the park?

Yes, there are several restaurants and snack bars within Terra Natura Benidorm offering a variety of dining options.

4. Are there any educational programs available?

Yes, Terra Natura Benidorm offers educational programs and interactive experiences for visitors of all ages. Check their website for more details.

5. Is there parking available at Terra Natura Benidorm?

Yes, there is ample parking available for visitors within the park premises.

6. Can I bring my own food and drinks into the park?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside Terra Natura Benidorm. However, there are designated picnic areas outside the park where you can enjoy your own food.

Yes, Terra Natura Benidorm offers discounted rates for group visits. Contact their customer service for more information on group bookings.
