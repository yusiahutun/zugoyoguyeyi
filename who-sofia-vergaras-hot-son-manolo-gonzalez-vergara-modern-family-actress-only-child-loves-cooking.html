Here’s what you need to know about the America’s Got Talent judge’s handsome son, Manolo Gonzalez Vergara:

Manolo Gonzalez Vergara is the son of Sofia Vergara and her childhood sweetheart Joe Gonzalez

Back in 1990, when Vergara was just 18, she married her childhood sweetheart Joe Gonzalez, according to Us Weekly.

She then gave birth to her first (and to date only) child, Manolo, a year later in 1991. However, the Colombian bombshell would call it quits just two years later, and by 1993 the pair had split.

Since becoming a Hollywood star, Vergara has been romantically linked to numerous high-profile men, from Tom Cruise and Mark Wahlberg to Enrique Iglesias, but she never wanted her son to be confused and always put him first, she told Parade in 2011, saying: “It’s OK for your kid to see you together on holidays, or going out for lunch or dinner, but that’s not the same as seeing him staying overnight in your room.”

He’s pursued acting roles

Like his famous mum, Manolo Gonzalez Vergara has tried his hand at acting too.

Per his IMDB profile, he starred in the film Hot Pursuit with his mum and Reese Witherspoon, and 2019 thriller Vandal, among others.

He launched a dog brand

The 32-year-old is also a doting dog dad and entrepreneur. Besides regularly sharing pics with pups on his social media, he runs a dog brand called Canini by Baguette (Baguette is his dog’s name), which sells everything from dog beds and leads to bandanas. The brand is doing so well it’s even available at Walmart.
