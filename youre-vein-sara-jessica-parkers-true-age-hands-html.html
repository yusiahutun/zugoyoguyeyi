She has made a career out of looking glamorous on and off screen. And at 49, Sarah Jessica Parker still has the looks to belie her age.

But even the Sex And The City star cannot escape some effects of ageing, it seems.

For while she may still have a complexion and body many younger woman would envy, her hands betray signs of her true age.

Not so handy: The hands belie Sarah Jessica Parker's youthful good looks at a New York gala on Tuesday

Attending an Aids charity gala in New York on Tuesday wearing a youthful Schiaparelli gown, Miss Parker covered her hands with rings and bangles – but they couldn’t distract attention from the wrinkles and prominent veins.

The problem – often caused by being underweight – puts her in glamorous company.

Angelina Jolie appeared at the Hollywood premiere of her latest movie, Maleficent, last month with bulging veins visible not only on her hands but also on her stick-thin arms.

Prominent: The veins on Angelina Jolie's hands at the première of Disney's Maleficent in Los Angeles

The 39-year-old actress, who at 5ft 7in weighs just 6st 9lb, is said to be considering laser surgery to cure the problem.

Madonna, 55, is another celebrity to suffer.

While the pop superstar works hard to maintain a youthful physique, photographs often reveal unsightly veins on her hands.
