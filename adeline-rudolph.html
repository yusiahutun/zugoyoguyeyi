Adeline Rudolph Bio, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth, wiki

Publish date: 2024-06-02

Adeline Rudolph is a German-Korean model and actress. She became extremely known internationally after her leading role, Agatha, in the Netflix series “Chilling Adventures of Sabrina”. Adeline also played Minerva Marble on “Riverdale”. She is now in the headlines and getting attention as she portrayed one of the main roles Billie Whesker in the ongoing “Resident Evil”.

Early Life

Adeline Rudolph was born on 10th February 1995 in Hong Kong. She also has a twin sister, Caroline Rudolph, and her mother is Korean while her dad is German. There aren’t many details about her parents, but Rudolph once disclosed in an interview that her parents were of Business background. She attended the German-Swiss International School in Hong Kong. According to various sites, she attended King George V Academy although, there is no proper information available about her graduation. After graduating from high school, she attended University College London and she studied Fashion Design. She graduated from the institution in 2007. Each year for summer vacations, she used to go to Germany, where her grandparents lived.

Adeline Rudolph Career

In an interview, she admitted that she didn’t plan on this career or modeling, she was just a typical girl. She modeled for her friends which she did for fun. But fate played its hand and many modeling agencies contacted her. And then she signed up with agencies such as Next Model Management, Los Angeles, PMA (Hamburg), and Primo in Hong Kong. She started charming people on the ramp. And then she became a well-known model and has been featured in many magazine editorials, like Elle, Fashion, and Marie Claire.
