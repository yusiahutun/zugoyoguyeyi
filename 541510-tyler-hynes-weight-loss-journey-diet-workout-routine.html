Who is Tyler Hynes?

Tyler Jeffrey Hynes was born on 6 May 1986 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, to Betty Robichaud Hynes (mother). He has never revealed his father's identity. Tyler was raised alongside his younger brother, Brandon Haynes. Brandon is a professional ice hockey player.

The Canadian celebrity went to St. Mother Teresa Catholic School for elementary education. He later attended St. Francis Xavier High School and North Dundas High School. Tyler made his acting debut in 1998 when he portrayed Demi Brooke in the hit family drama film Little Men.

The Canadian actor came into the limelight in 2019 after appearing in the TV series Letterkenny as Dierks. He has starred in popular TV shows and movies such as It Was Always You, Three Wise Men and a Baby, and Chimera.
