Bridgerton was a bright spot in 2020 and is now an even brighter one as the second season comes out tomorrow. The Duke of Hastings unexpectedly swept us away, along with Daphne, Eloise, Penelope and Lady Whistledown, of course. To welcome your fave characters back to the big screen, check out the following 15 gifts for Bridgerton fans.

The show combined the best of Jane Austen and Gossip Girl, while introducing us to the very handsome Regé-Jean Page and a reimagined Regency Era England. There was just so much to love, from the elegant and competitive balls to the fake-turned-real love match.

It’s unsurprising that Lady Whistledown and the gorgeous period costumes launched a cottage industry of Bridgerton gifts and even inspired some fashion trends. Daphne basically invented Bridgerton blue.

Whether you identify more as a Daphne or Penelope, there are gifts for everyone, including tea drinkers, aspiring Duchesses of Hastings and low-key writers. You can wear your love of Bridgerton on your chest, like Colin, or keep everything hidden, like the mysterious Lady Whistledown.

To celebrate the second season and get back into your fangirl feels, check out the cute and unofficial pieces of merch below.
