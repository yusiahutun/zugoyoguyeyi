Taylor Swift waited years for perfect response to Kanye interrupting her on stage

Publish date: 2024-02-27

Taylor Swift waited for the opportune moment to finally react to the Kanye West saga that sparked chaos at the VMAs. The iconic moment where she was interrupted on stage by the rapper, so he could rant about Beyoncé has been cemented into music history.

There isn’t a better celebrity feud than Tay-Tay and Ye. The Taylor-Kanye saga began at the 2009 MTV Video Music Awards ceremony, where the music legend received her first VMA award for You Belong With Me video. However, during Taylor Swift‘s speech for the Best Female Music Video gong, Kanye West surprised the audience by abruptly getting on stage. He took the microphone from her hands and started ranting about another star. He said: “I’ma let you finish, but Beyoncé had one of the best videos of all time! One of the best videos of all time!”

Taylor Swift had the most perfect comeback for Kanye West

Taylor Swift knows how to wait for Karma. The 12-time Grammy Awards-winning singer had the most genius comeback for Kanye interrupting on stage at the MTV VMAs. She waited to dive in during her very own history-making Eras tour concert.

When Taylor sat down to sing Champagne Problems at the Mexico City, fans interrupted her by pulling off a long TS chant.

Taylor was surprised by the fans’ love, quipped from the stage: “It’s the best way to be interrupted, by the way, just people chanting your name. I

“t’s really the only way to be interrupted… and I would know.”

Taylor extended an olive branch

Taylor Swift and Kanye West’s feud reached its peak during the infamous video controversy. However, before that, she extended an olive branch to the Donda crooner while receiving an award at the 2015 VMAs for her music video Bad Blood. This would have been six years after the awkward clip.

Taylor’s long speech on TV touched fans as she gushed: “I first met Kanye West six years ago… at this show, actually. Since then, we’ve had a lot of time to talk about a lot of different things.

“It seemed like everyone in the world knew about our infamous encounter at the VMAs, but something that you may not know is that Kanye West’s album, College Dropout, was the very first album that my brother and I ever bought on iTunes when I was 12 years old.

“Many of you may not remember that Kanye wrote and recorded his first single with his jaw wired shut after a car accident, or how he spent years studying the craft and the art of fashion and even learned to sew before launching his very own line that has been wildly successful.

“I have been a fan of his since I can remember, because Kanye defined what it is to be a creative force in music, fashion, and, well, life.”

Taking lines from Kanye’s lyrics, Taylor finished her speech with: “So I guess I have to say to all the other winners tonight, I’m really happy for you, and I’mma let you finish, but Kanye West had one of the greatest careers of ALL TIME.”
