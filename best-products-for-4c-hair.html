The Best Moisturizing Products for 4C Hair

Publish date: 2024-03-07

4C hair is fragile with tight, zig-zag curls that can appear "undefined" to the naked eye. And when detangling 4C curls, it takes time and gentle care, seeing as this hair type is prone to breakage. This is why it's vital to find the right products for this delicate texture, and thankfully, there are numerous products on the market that can leave your hair healthy and moisturized.

To help you find the best product for 4C hair, we researched various options on the market, considering their moisturizing, detangling, and softening abilities. With these features in mind, we found the 4C Only Too Easy Collection to be one of our favorite options.

Based on our research, here are the best products on the market for the softest 4C hair of your life.

4C Only Too Easy Collection

Available at 4conly and Amazon

While the natural haircare market constantly promotes type 3 hair, 4C Only is the first natural hair brand made specifically for those with a kinky-curly hair pattern. We love this collection from 4C Only, in particular, because it has everything you need to reset and strengthen your natural 4C hair.

Dubbed the Too Easy Collection, this line contains a shampoo, deep conditioner, leave-in conditioner, styling cream, and hair serum. Each product is packed with hair-loving plant oils and butters and will keep your hair clean, soft, detangled, and moisturized. What's more, the brand uses organic ingredients and keeps its labels simple, so you always know exactly what you're putting on your hair.

Although this is one of the more expensive items on our list, we recommend it for its high-quality ingredients and ease of use. However, if you're not ready to drop big dollars on your hair care just yet, at least invest in the Too Thicke Deep Conditioner from the line. It's super thick and guaranteed to penetrate curls (yes, even if you have low porosity hair). Sit under a heated dryer to help the product melt into your hair for even better results.

Mielle Organics Pomegranate & Honey Twisting Soufflé

Available at Target, Walmart, and Sallybeauty

To achieve a great twist-out and maintain your healthy hair, Mielle Organics Pomegranate & Honey Twisting Soufflé is the perfect product for your 4C texture. After you add your favorite leave-in conditioner (use something light), this creamy formula will lock moisture into your hair and give you a perfectly defined style. 4C naturals (including myself!) rave about the results.

Cream of Nature 7-in-1 Miracle Treatment

Available at Sallybeauty, Walmart, and Amazon

Cream of Nature 7-in-1 Miracle Treatment may have a new look, but this tried and true formula is a go-to for those in the 4C hair family. With argan oil from Morocco and vitamin B-5, the formula is creamy, yet doesn't weigh your hair down. It's an affordable multi-use product that will do everything from detangle and condition your hair to serve as a heat protectant. And if you are someone that doesn't like to have a bathroom full of hair products, this is a great one for 4C tresses, as it will moisturize, control frizz, and leave your hair with a luster. Thank us later.

Nexxus Curl Define Ultra-8 Pre-Wash Treatment

Available at Walgreens and CVS

Backed by research and development from Unilever scientists, in January Nexxus launched a customized range for curly hair. Nexxus Curl Define line has four products: a pre-wash treatment, shampoo, conditioner, and leave-in conditioner. The Curl Define Ultra-8 Pre-Wash Treatment doubles as a detangler and a mask. It's great for when you want to wash your day five braid-out or twist-out, making it easy to use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your style. The castor oil and shea butter give a perfect slip to detangle with ease and minimize breakage. It moisturizes and conditions your hair leaving it soft and hydrated. Oh, and if you're out of a co-wash, this product will can be used as a substitute.

Carol's Daughter Hair Milk Curl Refresher Spray

Available at Target, Amazon, and Walmart

Sometimes your 4C hair needs a mid-week boost. Carol's Daughter Hair Milk Curl Refresher is the perfect product to redefine your tresses without weighing them down. Several spritz of the agave nectar-packed formula will remoisturize your curls and get your hair through the week with style.

Dark and Lovely Protective Styles Tension Tamer

Available at GotoTarget, Walmart and Amazon

Even when your 4C hair is in a protective style, you still need to give it the necessary support. 4C hair is fragile, so if your braids or bantu knots feel too tight, soothe your scalp with Dark and Lovely Protective Styles Tension Tamer. Infused with peppermint oil, aloe, and avocado, the formula will give you immediate relief and the lotion is lighter than an oil, so it won't leave any residue behind on your scalp. The thin nozzle makes it easy to apply in between your braids and directly on your parts or even underneath a wig or weave.

As I Am Jamaican Black Castor Oil Shampoo

Available at Sallybeauty, Walmart and Amazon

Some shampoos will strip your hair of its natural oils but As I Am Jamaican Black Castor Oil Shampoo leaves your hair clean, fragrant, and moisturized. The product is lightweight enough for summer and won't weigh your hair down while in the winter it adds moisture and won't dry out your hair. For low porosity 4C curls, the creamy shampoo works up a lather and penetrates hair leaving it shiny. It provides great slip so you don't even have to wait for the conditioning process to detangle.
