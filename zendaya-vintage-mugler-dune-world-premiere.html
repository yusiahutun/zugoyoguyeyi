Zendaya wears iconic Mugler couture for 'Dune' premiere

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Zendaya is a regular on every best-dressed list and is always a highlight at any red-carpet event, now, her Dune: Part Two wardrobe is exceeding high expectations.

The Emmy-winning actor attended the sand-covered red carpet for the world premiere of the film – styled by Law Roach – wearing a piece of fashion history.

Her incredible vintage look was pulled from Mugler's autumn/winter 1995 ‘Cirque d’hiver’ 20th-anniversary collection archive, featuring a chrome suit of armour interlaced with clear panels across her body. Zendaya wore the look with an impressive diamond necklace with a large blue gemstone encrusted in the middle, as well as Mugler's matching metallic stiletto platform heels.

Daniel Leal//Getty Images

Getty Images

Getty Images

Zendaya later changed into another show-stopping Mugler look, this time in a floor-length black dress with a structured bodice. She wore it with vintage Bulgari jewellery, sourced from Ariel Saidian in New York.

Joe Maher//Getty Images

The star's suit of armour is just the latest in a long line of Dune press tour looks that her stylist Roach, lovingly referred to as “method dressing.”

“I think of it as method dressing, kind of like method acting,” Roach previously told Bazaar while discussing one of Zendaya's first major Dune looks: a dystopian-esque gown featuring large swaths of multi-coloured fabric wrapped around sculptural circular shapes. The dress is the brainchild of independent designer Torishéju Dumi.

“When I saw it after the designer showed it in Paris, I just kept it in the back of my head,” Roach continued. “I tried to see if we could modify it a little bit, and if you’ll notice, the skirt is a little bit different from how it showed on the runway.”
