Toblerone launches chocolate orange bar and fans say their lives have been made

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Toblerone is launching a new citrus flavour called Orange Twist.

The delicious treats combine Swiss milk chocolate, candied orange peel , ginger and honey & almond nougat.

They are currently on offer in Tesco, where you can pick up a 360g pack for £4 when ordering online.

This price will rise on September 8, when the cost will go up slightly to £4.50.

Chocoholics can’t wait to get their hands on the new Toblerone product – and many have taken to Twitter to share their excitement.

One commenter wrote: “OH MY GOODNESS. Two amazing things in one”, while a second said: “Omg I love Toblerone!!! And orange.”
