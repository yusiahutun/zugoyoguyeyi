Who is Paul Warfield:

Paul Warfield is a famous Football Player. He was born on November 28, 1942 and his birthplace is Warren, OH. Paul is also well known as, Hall of Fame wide receiver who played for the Cleveland Browns and Miami Dolphins. He was selected to 8 Pro Bowls during his career and won 2 Super Bowl Championships with the Dolphins.

Paul is originated from United States. In Super Bowl VI Richard Nixon called his coach to suggest a play for him to run.
