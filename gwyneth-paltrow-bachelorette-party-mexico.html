Gwyneth Paltrow's Bachelorette Party In Mexico With Celebrity Friends

Publish date: 2024-06-10

Bride-to-be Gwyneth Paltrow has been just been whisked off to Mexico by her famous friends for a VIP bachelorette party this weekend.

In January of this year, Paltrow and TV producer fiancé Brad Falchuk confirmed their engagement to Good Morning America. Now now, the Goop founder is celebrating her upcoming nuptials south of the border with her best (and famous) gal pals.

According to Page Six, Cameron Diaz orchestrated the event, chartering a private jet for 12 friends to fly down to Mexico for the party celebrating Paltrow.

Inside sources told the publication that guests will definitely include a few famous faces, like Diaz and fashion designer Stella McCartney. Paltrow's close friends include Drew Barrymore, Reese Witherspoon and (pregnant) Kate Hudson, but it's unconfirmed whether they're in attendance.

The group has an exciting weekend ahead. "They will be spending three days in Cabo before heading back to LA," a source told E! News. "They plan to spend their days at the beach and the spa. They also will go off site for dinner."

The 45-year-old actress touched down on Wednesday in vacation-ready style, wearing a chic and comfy floral jumpsuit, accessorized with a structured white-and-black Céline Phantom luggage bag and large-frame cat-eye sunglasses.

Splash News

Earlier this year, Paltrow admitted that planning her wedding with Falchuk has made her "feel like a 21-year-old" again.

“I’m excited about everything!” Paltrow told People following her engagement announcement. “I’ve never had a wedding before. [Paltrow and ex-husband, Coldplay's Chris Martin, eloped in 2003.] So even though I’m 45, I sort of feel like a 21-year-old.”

Splash News

The star and wellness entrepreneur also disclosed that all of her girlfriends are adorably invested in her wedding journey.

“It’s kind of fun to talk about with my girlfriends,” said Paltrow. “They send me pictures of dresses—they’re as excited about it as I am. It’s pretty cute.”

With a healthy amount of FOMO, we're sending well-wishes to Gwyneth and the girls celebrating in Mexico this weekend!
