Wrestler Hulk Hogan has finally sold his infamous 17,000-square-foot Gulf front estate for $6.2million, down more than $18million from his original asking price.

Hogan, whose real name is Terry Bollea, bought the land with his then-wife Linda in 1992 for $2million and built the house in 1996.

He put it on the market soon after Linda filed for divorce from him and a slew of costly lawsuits drained money from the wrestler.

Sold! Hulk Hogan's massive mansion was sold to an unnamed buyer for a paltry $6.2million, millions less than the original asking price

Lap of luxury: The mansion has five bedrooms, eight full baths and three half baths, a gym, and a rec room with its own kitchen

Spacious: The estate has more than 17,000 square feet on an acre and a half of land

The reality TV series Hogan Knows Best was filmed in the elaborate home.

Hulksanity: Hulk Hogan is famous for his moves in the ring, and his bleach-blonde mustache

Five years ago, Hogan originally listing the waterfront property for $25million, the Tampa Bay Times reported.

Due in part to the stagnant housing market, the reality star and wrestler had to dramatically lower the price.

According to Zillow, the home reached its peak work in December 2007, plummeting sharply – along with millions of other homes – as the housing bubble burst.

The identity of the cash buyer is not listed in Multiple Listing Service data.

The mansion has five bedrooms, eight full baths and three half baths, a gym, and a rec room with its own kitchen.

Recently, Hogan has appeared in promotional spots for Rent-A-Centre. In 2010, he even attempted a few fights in the ring.
