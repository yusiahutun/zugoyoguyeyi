Lifehouse Little Havana Hotel Review, Miami, Florida, United States

Publish date: 2024-07-14
Miami, Florida, United States

A design-forward boutique hotel inspired by the neighborhood’s Cuban heritage, Life House Little Havana is just a few blocks from famed Calle Ocho, boasting a rooftop bar that draws a local crowd. It offers a unique perspective on Miami for those interested in exploring beyond the tourist grounds of South Beach.
