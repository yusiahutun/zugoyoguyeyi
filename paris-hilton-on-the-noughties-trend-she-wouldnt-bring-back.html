Paris Hilton on the noughties trend she wouldn't bring back

Publish date: 2024-06-17

Paris Hilton, queen of noughties fashion, has revealed the one thing she wouldn't wear now from her heyday.

Known for her love for velour tracksuits and penchant for diamond embellishment, the socialite-cum-businesswoman says the trucker cap belongs in the past and should never be revived.

Getty Images

In an interview with Who What Wear, she was asked which trend from her Simple Life days that she would leave behind. "I would never wear a Von Dutch hat again even though I, like, wore them, like, every single day before," she said.

"That's, like, something I would not wear."

Despite making it clear that trucker caps won't be making a comeback, her 2000s style could well make a return via a collaboration with fast fashion online brand Boohoo, according to WWD. The line will pay homage to her most famous looks with a 70-piece collection, including her famous 21st birthday dress and slogan items with her catchphrase "that's hot."

Paris x Boohoo will launch globally on 20 June.
