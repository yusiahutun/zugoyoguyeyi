Zayn Malik assures fans he's okay after his foot was 'run over' at Paris Fashion Week

Publish date: 2024-03-07

Zayn Malik caused fans to go wild this weekend, as he made a rare public outing at Paris Men’s Fashion Week, but he may have rethought it for a second when his foot was seemingly run over…

Directioners went wild over the music star’s presence, as he stunned in two insane outfits. As you can imagine, crowds were going wild for the former One Direction star, and in the hustle and bustle, it looked like he may have gotten too close to one car.

Thousands of videos of the 31-year-old at the event surfaced online, as fans across the globe were in awe of how great the star looked.

However, there was one specific video that had loyal Directioners concerned. The video, circulating X, formerly known as Twitter, saw Zayn smiling as he walked through a crowd of fans on the streets of Paris, as he attended the Kenzo fashion show.

These smiles quickly turned upside down, as an array of voices shouting: “Be careful!” could be heard, as security ran to ‘protect’ the star, who could be seen limping for a second.

Of course, Directioners were concerned, but thankfully, the star took to Instagram to let everyone know, that his foot was all good.

In a photo dump from the night after the event, the star posted a number of pictures, including one of his white Kenzo sneakers, which had black tire marks on them.

In the caption, the Pillowtalk singer penned: “@kenzo @nigo Thanks for a great show! My foot is fine !! Thanks to my incredibly well-made shoes [laughing emoji, thumbs up emoji].”

Fans took to the comments as one joked: “Someone ran over your foot? That’s crazy because if you ran over me I’d say thank you.”

“Do you need someone to clean your shoes bae,” joked another. Honestly, we’d apply for that job.

The star’s style never left

Although it’s been a while since we’ve seen Zayn on a public outing, he hasn’t changed a bit. In Paris, he rocked blonde highlights at the tips of his spiked-up hair, similar to the Take Me Home era, in 2012.

His outfits were edgy, but effortlessly stylish as he rocked a matching geometric blue jacket and trousers at the Kenzo show.

Malik, who shares daughter Khai with ex, Gigi Hadid, treated fans to another public appearance on Saturday at the Valentino menswear fall/winter 2024-2025, with a unique blazer that had the words: “We’re so old, we have become young again,” printed across.

We love a stylish King!
