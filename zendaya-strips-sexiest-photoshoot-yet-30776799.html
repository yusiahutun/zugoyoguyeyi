Zendaya strips off for sexiest photoshoot yet in lace lingerie and tiny bikini

Publish date: 2024-04-06

Zendaya has posed for possibly her sexiest photoshoot yet and new snaps show the star in a backless dress, teeny bikinis and lace lingerie.

The former Disney Channel star posed for Elle and seriously turned up the heat in her super sexy ensembles.

In one of the snaps, Zendaya posed with her back to the camera as she placed her hands on her hips and turned her head to the side.

The slinky ensemble hugged every curve and covered her legs and arms as she posed.

Another picture showed Zendaya leaning on a ledge above a large blue pool as she gazed down the camera lens.

She wore a black lace bralette that peeked out from under her leather blazer as she placed both arms out by her sides.

The Euphoria actress wore a full face of makeup, complete with dark eyeshadow and red lipstick as she stared through her eyebrows.

In yet another gorgeous photo, Zendaya wore a bright red bikini underneath a floor-length white coat covered in fluff.

The swimsuit showed off her washboard stomach and incredible abs as she stuck one leg out behind the other.

Zendaya paired the look with black pointed toe heels as she worked the camera and posed in a white tiled bathroom, seemingly covered in blood.

The Dune actress’ pictures come after speaking to Elle about her life and career, which has skyrocketed in the last few years.

She touched upon the shift she felt back in 2022 and admitted: “After the last Spider-Man and the last season of Euphorie, there was a visceral change.”

“Before I could get away with going places and getting in and out. But in Boston, I would end up going right back home because it was really overstimulating.”

“Everybody would go hang out at a bar or something and I’d be like: ‘I’d love to but I think I could ruin everybody’s night because it’s just not going to be fun once I’m there.”
