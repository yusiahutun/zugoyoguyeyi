How to check your Roblox account value and where to sell

Publish date: 2024-09-02

Roblox is a collection of more than 50 million games created by its community of players. The creators get to create simple or complex games for the community. The more complex the game developed, the higher the chances of earning. So, how do you check your Roblox account value?

Gaming is fun, especially if you are getting paid for it. Most Roblox gamers are curious to know how they can get paid on the platform. Here are all the steps for checking your Roblox account value and where to sell it.

What is the value of my Roblox account?

You will need a calculator and a Roblox trading website to check your Roblox account value. There are many reliable trading websites online that you can choose from to be your Roblox account checker.

After settling on a website, you will be required to sign in and verify your Roblox account on the trading website.

After signing in, you will need to navigate to the Account option on the taskbar and then select “verify your account” from the drop-down menu. You can then proceed to search for your account by typing in your username.

You will then be provided with a Verification Phrase. You will be required to copy and paste the phrase to your Roblox profile. As soon as the verification process is done, you will be able to view your Roblox profile through the trading website.

The website will display your Roblox account value, your collectibles, and your rank in the game.

The value of your Roblox account will be the amount displayed under “Value” on your profile. It is important to note that this value does not equate to your total Roblox worth.

The Roblox account value represents all the items you own in Roblox, excluding items you may have purchased in Roblox.

How do I see how much my Roblox account is worth?

In order to check the worth of your Roblox account, you will have to visit the inventory of your account, visible on the left side. The items on your account are in categories and subcategories according to their types for easy access.

You will see all your non-limited and limited items with their stated price in the different categories. This is where you will need your calculator to manually add the value of each item. Add all the non-limited items that you have on your Roblox account and note down the value.

Now sum up the value of your limited and non-limited items, then add the total to your Robux value. The Robux amount can be found on your Robux profile. The total of these values will tell you the worth of your Roblox account.

Can you sell your Roblox account?

As a creator or player on Roblox, you are allowed to sell the games you have created or purchase games from creators. Roblox frowns upon the idea of creators selling their accounts, though.

Selling a Roblox account is against the Terms of Service of Roblox. The terms are strict on this to the extent that it states sharing a Roblox account is not allowed.

Selling or sharing an account can lead Roblox to permanently ban your account. However, this has not stopped creators from selling their accounts. Selling accounts is not a criminal offense; therefore, most creators have used that loophole to sell accounts and get away with it.

Also, Roblox does not have a method of tracking creators who sold their old accounts and created new ones. The downside of selling your account is that you may incur charges because you have to sell it through a website at a fee.

Older accounts and high levels of ranking tend to make a lot of money on Roblox. So it is important to remain active by trying out new games, creating items, or joining groups.
