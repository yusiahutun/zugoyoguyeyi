American singer, Sierra Deaton crossed the borderline of popularity as one half of the indie pop group, Alex & Sierra.

She entered into The X Factor with her boyfriend and claimed the title of The X Factor USA 2003, which made their bond stronger. Also, they had planned on getting married, but before reaching their goal, they split their ways.

Sierra Deaton Bio

Deaton is a well-known singer, songwriter, and dancer. She was born on 11 February 1991 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. She was raised by her father, Dr. John Deaton, and mother, Vu Deaton, in Orlando, Florida. John Deaton possesses British descent, while Vu Deaton claims Vietnamese descent. She has an older sister, Lara Deaton.    

Sierra started dancing at the early age of 2. She had studied hip hop, jazz, ballet, modern, tap, and more. Giving you a taste of her dancing skills, she has won 3 international titles in Irish dancing, competing for eleven years. 

For her education, she went to Lake Howell High School. She then studied advertising and public relation at The University of Central Florida and graduated with honors in 2013.
