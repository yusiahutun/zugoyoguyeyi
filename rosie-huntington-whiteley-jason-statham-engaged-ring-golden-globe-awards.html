Rosie Huntington-Whiteley &amp; Jason Statham Are Engaged -- Debuts Gorgeous Ring at Golden Globe Awards

Publish date: 2024-09-11

Rosie Huntington-Whiteley and Jason Statham are engaged!

The couple's reps confirmed the news to E! after the pair walked the red carpet at the 2016 Golden Globe Awards, where the 28-year-old stunner debuted her gorgeous ring with her fiance by her side.

The model wowed in a gorgeous gold beaded Atelier Versace gown and was all smiles as she walked the carpet with the "Spy" actor. She kept her hair in loose waves and finished her look with flawless makeup, and her gorgeous diamond sparkler!

Her other half looked dapper in a velvet tuxedo jacket, bow tie and black dress pants.

The two are notoriously private about their relationship, but the "Transformers 3" star got candid in an interview with Self last year about the possibility of marriage and kids with her then-beau.

"For any couple that's been together for a certain amount of time, there's a massive amount of pressure to get married," she said. "I totally believe in marriage, but being in a happy relationship is much more important to me... [Kids are] something that' s more realistic in the near future."

The husband and wife-to-be have been together for five years and we can't wait to see what Rosie will look like on her big day!

Congrats to the happy couple!
