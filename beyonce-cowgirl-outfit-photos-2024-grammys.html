Beyonc Makes a Grand Appearance As a Glamorous Cowgirl at the 2024 Grammys

Publish date: 2024-02-09

As if the 2024 Grammys couldn’t get more grand, Beyoncé made a last-minute appearance.

The superstar snuck into Sunday night’s ceremony in L.A. dressed like a glamorous cowgirl. Photos from inside the event show her at a table with husband Jay-Z and older daughter Blue Ivy.

Her ensemble consisted of a black leather jacket embellished with silver studs in a checkered pattern all over, layered over matching shorts and a crisp white collared shirt with a black tie. She finished the outfit with a white cowgirl hat by Stetson, pointy black pumps with ankle straps, a black Louis Vuitton belt with a silver buckle featuring the fashion house’s logo, and various diamond rings.

Beyoncé’s blonde hair was slicked down into a super-straight ’do, and she wore rosy makeup and a red manicure.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Next to her, Jay-Z looked cool in an all-black suit and black button-down, plus shiny black dress shoes.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Blue looked like a princess in a white ball gown with a metallic sheen—an ode to her mom’s Renaissance album and tour, which the 12-year-old performed on as a dancer. The dress featured an off-the-shoulder sweetheart neckline and an ample skirt. Blue paired it with chunky white boots with platform heels and wore full glam, including a red lip; she had her hair styled down in sleek, long braids.

VALERIE MACON//Getty Images

While Beyoncé was not nominated for any Grammys this year, Jay-Z was honored with the Dr. Dre Global Impact Award for his contributions to the music industry. The rapper got onstage with his daughter to accept the award, and gave a passionate speech about the award show and the larger meaning behind the Grammys.
