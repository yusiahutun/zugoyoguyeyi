Samaria Leah Wisdom Smith Age & Biography

Samaria was born on September 15, 1995, to James Todd Smith (LL Cool J) and Simone Smith & is currently 27 years old.

She graduated from Campbell High School and earned a Bachelor’s degree from US State University. She began modeling at Karl Kani after graduating from high school. Samaria also earned two degrees from the Fashion Institute of Technology (FIT). The first was in Fashion Design, and the second in Fashion Merchandising.

Samaria has always resisted her mother’s attempts to dress her as a child, according to her official business page. She changed her clothes three to five times per day.

Samaria Leah Wisdom Smith Parents

Samaria Todd (LL Cool J) is the second child of James Todd and Simone Smith. They first met in 1987 and married in 1995.

In an Interview, James revealed that the couple met through Simone’s cousin for the first time. LL Cool J was driving his mother’s car on Easter. He then noticed his friend and pulled over to say hello.

After greeting his friend, LL Cool J was invited to meet his cousin. Simone is the wife of LL Cool J, a well-known rapper and producer. She is a jewelry designer who was born on March 31, 1968. Simone owns a Charitable Jewelry Line named the Sweet Touch Of Hope.

LL Cool J (Father)

James Todd Smith, better known as LL Cool J, is an American rapper, actor, songwriter, and record producer. James is an entrepreneur as well as a well-known personality. He owns the Todd Smith clothing line, which sells urban wear. He also launched TROOP, a hip-hop-themed sportswear line.

In Queens, New York, James Smith established the Jump & Ball charitable foundation. This foundation organizes athletic and team-building activities.

LL Cool J was raised in St. Albans, Queens, by his grandparents. He began rapping at the age of ten, inspired by the hip-hop group The Treacherous Three.
