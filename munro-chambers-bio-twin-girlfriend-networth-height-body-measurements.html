Munro Chambers Bio, Twin, Girlfriend, Networth, Height, Body Measurements

Publish date: 2024-05-29

Munro Chambers, (born in Munro Skylear Chambers), is an established Canadian actor whose acting skills simply cannot be questioned.

He is famous for his roles as Wilder in The Latest Buzz (2007-2010), Elijah “Eli” Goldsworthy in Degrassi (2010-2015), Frankie Chandler in Lockwood (since 2006), and Nate in Second Jen (since 2006).

Munro’s star sign is Leo.

Munro Chambers Bio, Twin

Munro Chambers was born on July 29, 1990, in Ajax, Ontario, Canada, the son of Scott and Lorette Chambers. He has a twin brother, Thomas, also an actor who is two minutes older than him and also a younger brother named Michael.

Munro Chambers and his twin brother attended the local high school Newmarket High School in Newmarket, Ajax, alongside Charlotte Arnold, who plays Holly J. Sinclair in Degrassi- The Next Generation.

They (the Chambers twins) currently attend the University of Ontario. Munro was a nationally recognized trampoline player together with his brother Thomas. They grew up in a simple middle-class family and their talent made them very popular.

Munro Chambers was one of the presenters for the Much Music Video Awards 2011. He is best friends with Justin Kelly and Demetrius Joyette, his co-stars from The Latest Buzz. His uncle is Colin Mochrie, from the American version of Whose Line Is It Anyway?

The twin brothers developed an interest in music and acting at an early age, which is why Munro began his acting career at the tender age of eight.

His first “acting” job was a McDonald’s commercial, while his first real acting experience came in 1998 when he played the supporting role of Rob in the TV series “Little men”.

Munro also performed the song “Songbirds” together with Danielle Belanger at Newmarket Theatre.

In 2003 he appeared in the children’s fantasy movie “A Wrinkle in Time” as Sandy Murray. In the same year, he also played the role of Billy in the movie “Good Fences”.

After that Munro Chambers became actively involved in the entertainment industry and in 2004 he played Max Shaw in the movie “Godsend”.

Still, in 2004, he took part in the television series “Still Game” with a supporting role in Jack.

In 2005 the talented actor appeared as Greg Ammon in the thriller “Murder in the Hamptons”.

From 2007 – 2010 Munro played the character, Wilder, in the TV series “The Latest Buzz”, which brought him more popularity.

From 2010 on he was a supporting character in the TV series “Degrassi” for five years. Munro had originally auditioned for the role of “Drew” before he got the role of “Eli” in the series.

In 2011 he appeared in the family comedy “Beethoven’s Christmas Adventure” alongside John Cleese.

Three years later, Munro Chambers was engaged with episodic roles in a few TV series such as “The Stanley Dynamic” and “The Divide”.

In 2015, the born actor made a big breakthrough with the role of the Kid in the movie “Turbo Kid”. The leading role gave him a huge new level of fame.

Munro also played Frankie Chandler in the television series “Lockwood” in 2016 and appeared as Teddy in the film “Sadie’s Last Days on Earth”, which was released on December 9, 2016.

Due to the fact that Munro Chambers is an actor who is undeniably good at what he does, the young guy was nominated for the Canadian Screen Award for Best Performance in 2014 and was also nominated for the Shorty Award and the Gemini Award in 2011.

In 2016, Munro won the Jury Award for Best Actor Turbo Kid (2015) at the Victoria TX Indie Film Festival.
