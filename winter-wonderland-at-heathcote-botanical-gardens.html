Winter Wonderland at Heathcote Botanical Gardens

Publish date: 2024-02-17

Explore five acres of Heathcote Botanical Gardens glammed up for the holidays during Garden of Lights. The magical display begins Thanksgiving weekend (November 27-28), and guests can explore the decorated grounds the following Fridays and Saturdays through January 2. In addition to the whimsical, dazzling lights, guests can sample bites from food trucks and take advantage of photo ops with the gardens’ mascot, Heathcote the Bear. $8/adults, $5/members, $3/children ages 6-12; 210 Savannah Road, Fort Pierce; (772) 464-4672
