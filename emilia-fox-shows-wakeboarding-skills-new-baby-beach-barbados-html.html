She only gave birth to baby Rose two months ago - but Silent Witness star Emilia Fox showed she was back in action in every which way on her Barbados beach holiday yesterday.

The 36-year-old beauty has not only managed to win the battle of the bulge and snap back to her pre-baby figure, but she showed that she could conquer the waves too with an impressive display of wakeboarding.

Emilia's boyfriend Jeremy Gilley meanwhile was left on the beach literally holding the baby as mummy showed off her skills in the tough watersport, that's a cross between waterskiing, windsurfing and snowboarding.

All action: Emilia Fox donned another pair of baggy polka dot bikini bottoms to go wakeboarding in Barbados

Beach babe: Beautiful little baby Rose is cuddled by father Jeremy Gilley as she enjoys her first ever holiday

The couple welcomed their new addition into the world in November -  three years after Emilia, then with her ex-husband, Mad Men actor Jared Harris, suffered a miscarriage. They split in 2008.

She then met actor, film-maker and founder of the charity Peace One Day Gilley at the beginning of 2009, not long after her marriage had broken down.

Emilia has previously said of her man: 'When I met him at the Peace One Day concert, I was in a world of my own, preoccupied by what was going on with me and Jared, so I didn't compute that I was attracted to him until much later.'

She continued: 'The relationship I had with Jared is incomparable to this. When you break up with someone you thought you were going to be with for the rest of your life, it's sad.'

She has also said she had 'no idea' whether she would ever marry again.

Married or not, the pair are clearly exceptionally in love and are thoroughly enjoying their first family holiday - even if Emilia clearly isn't someone to spend her time lying around on the beach.

Emilia is clearly a fan of wakeboarding as she was spied out on the surf tackling the sport on Monday - but her equally baggy bikini bottoms then almost caused the actress some at-sea embarrassment.

Bikini malfunction: Silent Witness actress Emilia's bikini bottoms started to slip down as she went wakeboarding in Barbados on Monday

As the star sped across the waves pulled by a speed boat, gravity almost won with the bottom half of her white and black polka dot swimsuit.

Emilia, who plays Dr Nikki Alexander in hit BBC show Silent Witness, recently said she is already keen to add to her brood.

Surfs up: Fox looks like she's enjoying herself as she zips across the waves at high speed

After hearing the news that Victoria and David Beckham are expecting a fourth child, Emilia said: 'I want many more babies. Having done it once I want to do it again and again and again.

'I had such a great time in labour, it was quite an extraordinary experience with a wonderful midwife. It was very painful at the time but as soon as the baby was out it was great.'


Wave rider: Action girl Emilia carries the board into the Caribbean sea

But while Emilia is keen to extend her family with Jeremy, she has very specific ideas of what career her children should follow, and is hopeful Rose won't follow her into the acting industry.

Emilia was pregnant while filming the current series of Silent Witness, but she says she hopes her daughter picked up more of the medical terminology while in the womb than the acting side.

Beach body: Emilia showed off her incredible figure just two months after giving birth to daughter Rose

Toned curves: Emilia is clearly already back to her pre-pregnancy figure, which she proved by wearing a cute maroon and black bikini

She said: 'Apparently babies can hear in the womb, so I just hope that the baby's been picking up on the pathology and not the fun of acting and that she'll head straight to medical school.'

Acting is in the genes, however. Emilia was born into an acting dynasty - her mother is Joanna David, her father Edward Fox, and her cousin Laurence Fox is the star of Lewis and husband of actress Billie Piper.

Brother Freddie is also just beginning to spread his acting wings, with roles in Any Human Heart and Worried About The Boy.
