3.1 Phillip Lim Apex for Youth Masks

19

3.1 Phillip Lim Apex for Youth Masks

Credit: 3.1philliplim.com

Phillip Lim and his team have galvanized their efforts to support Apex for Youth, an organization that offers mentorship and resources to underserved, low-income Asian and immigrant youth in NYC. Sold in bundles of five or ten, with children and adult sizing options, the masks are equipped with antibacterial Fuze Pathogen Control technology. While not to be used as a substitute for medical-approved masks, the bacteria-fighting properties strengthen with washing, as dirt particles are removed. The NYC fashion house donated the first 1,000 masks directly to the nonprofit, and 100 percent of all proceeds from purchases will go to the organization.
