Zendaya showcases her best 'tennis look' yet in plunging green dress - It's 'to die for'

Publish date: 2024-09-14

Zendaya’s tennis-themed outfits are just getting better, and we think her recent neon green dress may have taken the crown.

The Hollywood actress has been traveling across the globe promoting her latest movie, Challengers, and if there was an award for best dressed, she’d be winning every time. The 27-year-old’s stunning look was debuted at an LA Afterparty, and we’re sure Tom Holland is furious he couldn’t be there to witness it in real life. He’ll just have to fangirl over the pics like the rest of us…

Zendaya smashes the tennis look with stunning neon green dress

All eyes were on the Euphoria star at the Challengers after-party in LA, as guests would have seen her from a mile off in her custom Celia Kritharioti bright green dress.

The plunging number showed off the star’s stunning figure, equipped with a remarkable thigh high split, which may quickly be becoming her signature look.

Of course, it wouldn’t be a Zendaya look if there wasn’t something unique attached. At the bottom of the plunging neckline was a cute tennis ball tying it all together. We are obsessed!

After showing off her blonde locks as of late, the Spider-Man star decided to switch things up a bit, heading back to her brunette roots with a neat bun.

The floor-length dress was so elegantly long, but her white stilettos poked out as she walked around the venue. We were hoping for the tennis ball shoes, but that may have been just a bit too much!

“The tennis ball! This is so cute!!!” exclaimed one fan on X, formerly known as Twitter.

“This look is to die for,” wrote another. “Zendaya always understands the assignment.” Yes, she does!

It must be exhausting serving so many looks, but someone’s got to do it, right? Before her stunning neon gown, Zendaya took to the LA premiere, and once again, understood the assignment.

The star stunned in a black Vera Wang corset gown with a dramatic pink skirt, and once again, absolutely slayed the look. No tennis theme here, but no one was complaining about that!

“She’s an icon, she’s a legend and she is the moment!” exclaimed one fan upon seeing the breathtaking look.

“That is so elegant and gorgeous. She is everything,” wrote another.

A third wrote: “She could wear anything, even a trash and she would still look gorgeous.”
