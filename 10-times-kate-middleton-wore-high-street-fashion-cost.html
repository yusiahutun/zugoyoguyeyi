10 times Kate Middleton wore high street fashion that cost less than US$100, from her Zara dress bef

Publish date: 2024-09-16
As the newly married Middleton left for her honeymoon, she wore a blue pleated Zara dress that cost just under US$90. The dress is no longer available on Zara’s website, but Fashionista quoted its retail price.

When Middleton wore a US$50 polka dot dress from Topshop to visit Warner Bros. Studios, it sold out within an hour of her appearance. She also wore the Topshop frock to Prince George’s godfather’s wedding.

She wore a Seraphine dress while visiting her family home in Berkshire after having Prince George. The pink fuchsia knot front maternity dress retails for US$75.

She wore another dress from Zara at a charity polo match. While Prince William played in the Maserati Royal Charity Polo Trophy, Middleton ran after Prince George and Princess Charlotte in the flowing blue and white striped dress, which cost US$70. The dress, with gathered seams at the front and back and a tie at the waist, is no longer available.

Some royal engagements call for ball gowns, others call for sturdy jeans and boots. To visit a farm in rural England, Middleton wore US$50 pants from Zara. The olive green mid-rise biker jeans that Middleton wore to visit Farms for City Children are no longer in stock.

She made a relaxed boyfriend shirt from Gap look tailored and polished on a visit to New Zealand. The shirt usually sells for US$54.95, but was on sale for US$23.97 and quickly sold out.

Middleton chose a polka dot wrap dress from Asos for a visit to a children’s charity in London. The Asos maternity Kate wrap skater dress, now named for the duchess, is currently out of stock on their website. It retails for US$53 at full price and US$39 on sale.

Middleton’s official portrait was unveiled at the National Portrait Gallery in 2014. When sitting for the portrait, Marie Claire reported that she wore a US$75 French Connection navy sub silky tie top that is no longer available.

Middleton’s purple Warehouse jumper, worn while visiting London’s Natural History Museum in October 2019, retails for US$69. According to the fashion blog Kate’s Closet, the Warehouse jumper features a pointelle texture and scalloped edges. Middleton wore the jumper with green wide-leg pants from Jigsaw that normally retail for US$133. They went on sale for US$39, but they are currently sold out.

She wore a blazer from Zara for St. Patrick’s Day in 2021. The double-breasted textured weave jacket in apple green retails for US$89.90.
