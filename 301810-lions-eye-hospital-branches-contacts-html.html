Lions Eye Hospital branches and contacts

Publish date: 2024-03-14

The full name of Lions Eye Hospital is Lions Sight-First Eye Hospital (LSEH). It is a medically approved institution by the Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Board as a specialist training centre. LSEH started operating in 1997 and operates as a charitable organization that provides quality services and treatment to people from all socio-economic backgrounds. The hospital aims to have a rural and urban outreach to deliver its services to the people regardless of their financial capability.

LSEH as an organisation has specialized in eye health clinical services, education, rehabilitation, sight enhancement, and an eye bank. The main agenda is to deliver free eye and health services to the financially stricken people, especially those dwelling in rural areas and have a challenge accessing or even affording quality eye care.

LSEH has grown exponentially, spreading its services across the East Africa region. Over 70% of the services offered to the general public are free.

Lions Eye Hospital services

The services on offer capsulate different health concerns and it is great to know that patients can access health solutions at relatively affordable prices. The prices are discounted to ensure that many people can access medical services.

Listed are the specialist eye care LSEH offers are:

  • Cataracts
  • Refractive errors
  • Glaucoma
  • Squint
  • Uveitis
  • Computer
  • Vision syndrome
  • Ptosis
  • Pediatric Ophthalmology
  • Orbit and Oculoplasty
  • Low vision
  • Age-related Macular degeneration
  • Amblyopia

What else does LSEH do?

Despite being an eye clinic, LSEH has established itself as a fully-fledged hospital that provides other health services to the general public. They include:

  • Dental services
  • Renal services, are kidney related illnesses including kidney failure and dialysis
  • Ear services covering the deaf, partially deaf and people with hearing ailments
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Laboratory services

LSEH offers a community outreach program that helps in the spread and awareness of preventive measures and cure as well. The hospital engages the public at a community level to help advance the populations human resources through training.

This branch of services operates under the watch of Kenya medical practitioners and dentists’ board. It has been operational since November 2005. The courses on offer are:

  • Small incision manual cataract surgery course
  • Community outreach and social marketing eye care services
  • Ophthalmic Technician course

Lions Eye Hospital diabetes care center

The core of the centre dwells in providing holistic multidisciplinary care to patients and families. The focus is on discovering preventive measures and cure for all people suffering and living with diabetes. As such, LSEH takes part in creating awareness of diabetes to people who are affected with diabetes either directly or indirectly.

This program ensures that people diagnosed with diabetes can practice self-management and minimize all risks that can develop from diabetes-related complications.

Lions Eye Hospital charges

Upon visiting this organisation's website, you will note that it offers its services at subsidized rates so as to serve as many people as possible. The organisation works and partners with well-wishers and donours to get funds to help them operate. Also, the hospital accepts insurance cards from many major insurers as well as the National Health Insurance Fund just in case you were searching for Lions Eye Hospital NHIF.
