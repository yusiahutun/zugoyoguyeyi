Naomi Watts' effortlessly chic Feud Capote wardrobe

Publish date: 2024-07-18

Carolina Herrera

Gotham//Getty Images

The actress was seen out in New York wearing this beautiful black silky look by Carolina Herrera, featuring a strapless top and wide-legged trousers.

Brandon Maxwell

Ignat/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Naomi put a fresh spin on the trouser suit, in a crisp white two-piece by Brandon Maxwell, worn with matching white Jimmy Choo pumps, a black Gabriela Hearst bag and square-frame sunglasses.

Miu Miu

Getty Images

She paired this shimmering sheer Miu Miu look with Wolford tights and platform heels – the perfect winter party outfit – to appear on The Tonight Show.

Balmain

Getty Images

Watts put her best fashion foot forward in a pair of black Giuseppe Zanotti platform heels, worn with a chic pencil skirt and an Eighties-inspired silk shirt with padded shoulders, by Balmain.

Bottega Veneta

Jose Perez/Bauer-Griffin

The star accessorised her cream Bottega Veneta skirt and cosy black coat with Celine's Classique 16 bag. Timeless dressing at its finest.

Tory Burch

Getty Images

For another press outing, the actress was seen wearing a military-style Tory Burch coat, accessorised with Krewe sunglasses, a goes-with-everything handbag from New York-based brand Nili Lotan and black pumps from Vivaia, an eco-footwear label.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Celine

Getty Images

Perhaps our favourite ensemble from her stylish wardrobe so far was this sheer Celine slip dress. She added an elegant touch to the dress with red lipstick, Tiffany & Co earrings and a sleek side-parting.

Givenchy couture

Gotham//Getty Images

The actress turned to Givenchy's archives for the Feud premiere at New York's Museum of Modern Art, in a lace gown taken from the fashion house's spring/summer 2019 couture catwalk show. She accessorised with matching opera gloves, a Judith Leiber swan-shaped crystal clutch bag and Tiffany & Co. jewellery.
