Christina El Moussa Posts Bikini Photos From Pool Day at Her Home

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Christina El Moussa is keeping cool by the pool!

The Flip or Flop host celebrated the end of summer with a relaxing day at her and husband Ant Anstead’s backyard retreat. The couple are expecting their first child together, a son, any day now, so Christina definitely deserved a little R&R.

Christina El Moussa/Instagram

She showed off her baby bump in a black bikini and hat with gold details. She kicked back on a bright pink float that we’re adding to our carts ASAP for our next vacation.

Christina El Moussa/Instagram

Christina’s other activity that day was to watch her and ex-husband Tarek El Moussa’s kids, Taylor and Brayden, zip down the slide and splash around in the water. Ant even got in on the fun, letting Brayden ride on his back while Christina and their pup, Cash, captured the cute moment from the sidelines.

Christina El Moussa/Instagram

Christina has been poolside for much of her pregnancy, and we can see why. It’s practically a spa back there, complete with lounge chairs, cabanas, a hot tub, and a fire pit. Seriously, why does she ever leave the house?

The Christina on the Coast star will have a very good reason to soon though. She recently announced she and Ant decided on a “scheduled C-section” for their little one, and are expecting him in early September.

We can’t wait to meet baby Anstead—and we’re sure he’ll love his sweet backyard digs just as much as his mom!
