Youre on BBC1, not Babestation 400 moan over Ora boobs but shouldn't they get a life?

Publish date: 2024-04-12

A whopping 400 complaints piled in after the Hot Right Now singer appeared on The One Show wearing a plunging peek-a-boob neckline.

Now the show has issued an apology after viewers moaned about the talent show judge’s cleavage following her appearance on the show on Monday night.

The R.I.P hitmaker caused a stir as she chatted to hosts Matt Baker and Alex Jones – alongside her fellow Voice coaches Tom Jones, Ricky Wilson and Will.i.am – having replaced Kylie Minogue for the fourth series which kicks off on Saturday.

She wore her white Ermanno Scervino trouser suit with nothing underneath the blazer, daring to flaunt that 50 per cent of the population have.

One enraged viewer posted on a BBC messageboard: "Isn't it about time the BBC had a dress code?

"I do not want to see her boobs hanging out on a family programme. I find it quite disgraceful."

Another tweeted: "Can't believe Rita Ora is wearing that on The One Show. It's 7pm BBC1 love not Babestation at 2am."

As a result of the furore, The One Show posted a message on their Facebook page to those who were "offended" by Rita’s display.

"We’re sorry to those of you who were offended by Rita Ora’s choice of outfit on yesterday’s show," they wrote.

"If we had been consulted on it we would have requested she wore something more suitable for 7pm."

Really, are they her mum now?

However, a BBC spokesperson had a different approach to the situation saying that they didn’t believe the 24-year-old’s outfit was inappropriate.

"The One Show allows guests to choose their own attire and pop stars often opt for something glamorous or striking," they said.

"We didn't feel that Rita's outfit would be outside of most viewers' expectations of that of a major star, but we appreciate that tastes vary."

Better check with Antiques Road Trip if guests are allowed to wear an over-the-knee skirt.
