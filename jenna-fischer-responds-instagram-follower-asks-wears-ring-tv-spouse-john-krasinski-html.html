Jenna Fischer responds after Instagram follower asks if she wears ring from TV spouse John Krasinski

Publish date: 2024-04-23

Jenna Fischer responded to an admittedly 'weird question' on Instagram after posting an upbeat photo while in quarantine with her husband.

The 46-year-old actress shared a photo Sunday of herself and husband Lee Kirk enjoying a 'Shelter-In Date' in their yard that included coffee and bird watching.

A follower posted in the comments, 'Yooo, do you still wear that ring john krasinski gave you??? It's a weird question but I kept hearing that you do. Or did'.

Weird question: Jenna Fischer shared a photo of herself with husband Lee Kirk enjoying their yard on Instagram on Sunday that prompted an admittedly 'weird question' from a follower

Jenna portrayed Pam Beesly in The Office while John, 40, played her love interest Jim Alpert and their characters got married on the NBC show.

The ring rumor dates back to a seven-year-old Reddit thread.
