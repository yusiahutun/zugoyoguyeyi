They've been inseparable ever since they started dating in October.

And Taylor Swift and Jake Gyllenhaal wasted no time getting back into each other's arms after spending a week apart.

While Jake was in Sydney promoting new film Love & Other Drugs, Taylor was in Nashville working on some new music.

Together again: Taylor Swift and Jake Gyllenhaal went for a drive on Thursday after they both returned to Los Angeles

And as soon as they were both back in town, Jake picked Taylor up in his Audi Q7.

Taylor, who turns 21 next week, busied herself by playing with her iPhone while 29-year-old Jake navigated the traffic in Beverly Hills.

The couple seem to be going from strength to strength, with sources close to the couple confirming that it's a serious relationship.

'Taylor is smitten. She loves how nice and affectionate he is,' an insider tells Us Weekly magazine.

Busy girl: Taylor occupies herself by playing with her iPhone while Jake is busy driving

'Jake likes that Taylor is sweet, low-key and very easy to be around.'

The singer opened up recently about her upcoming birthday and how excited she is to finally be able to legally go out and have fun.

'I don't think I'm going to all of the sudden start being in love with the idea of going to a club,' she tells People magazine.

'I like to dance around and have fun with my friends and we have a blast, but I've never really felt compelled to be drunk and I don't think that's terribly awful.'

Onwards and upwards: Jake and Taylor have been dating for six weeks

And while she isn't against having the occasional drink, it's concerts she's most looking forward to.

She says: 'It'll be cool that I'll be able to go to a lot of concerts that my friends always go to and I sit at home because it's 21 and over.'

As for the birthday party itself, Taylor wants a Christmas-themed party.

'I definitely want a winter-themed birthday -- Christmas-themed. And I'd really like to have it in my condo. I think that would be wonderful.'
