Kali Reis is a known American personality, professional boxer, and female actor. She has won the title of two weight classes. Kali participated in the WBC female middleweight title in 2016 and the WBA, WBO, and IBO female light welterweight titles between 2020 and 2022. She has received both titles and has been the champion in weightlifting for years.

Kali Reis was born on 24th August 1986, making her 38 years old as of 2024. Kali Reis is the youngest among all five children born to her parents. She and her siblings were single-handedly raised by her mother in East Providence, Rhode Island. When she was a child, she used to participate in singing, dancing, and other various competitions. She has received the nickname “K.O. Mequinonoag.” The word Mequinonoag means “many feathers” or “many talents.” This name was given to her by her mother.

During childhood, she often played athletics with her neighborhood boys. She also participated in the school’s marching or parade bands, color guards, and volleyball teams. During her junior year in high school, she was active in basketball and softball leagues. She was 14 when she began boxing at Manfredo’s Gym in Pawtucket, Rhode Island. At the gym, she received coaching from her mother’s friend. She went to the school of criminology and learned how to repair coaching at MTTI.

Kali Reis went or continued to receive her training at the boxing gym and joined Big Six Boxing Academy in Providence. Before completing her university or educational career, she secured various titles like the 2006 New England 154 Championship, the 2007 NYC Golden Gloves, and the 2007 Rocky Marciano Championship. Besides boxing and acting career, she is an active supporter of the Missing and Murdered Indigenous Women and Girls (MMIWG) movement.

Boxing Career

Kali Reis began her boxing career in childhood but met an accident in 2012. Eventually, she had to get sidelines from her boxing career. Kali then returned to boxing in 2013 and participated in the WIBA competition that year in November. The way she made a comeback in boxing, she received immense limelight. Then, in November 2014, Kali Reis received the IBA title for another boxing match. She has now become a coach and trained many students for boxing and other games.

Acting Career

She debuted acting in 2021 and worked for an American thriller, Catch the Fair One. She portrayed the role of a boxer rescuing her missing sister. She has received a nomination for the leading role or best female by the Independent Spirit Awards. She has also worked on a mystery series, True Detectives: Night Country, released on 14th January 2024.
