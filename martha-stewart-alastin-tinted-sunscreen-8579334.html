Martha Stewart Revealed the Skincare Product She Uses Every Morning

Publish date: 2024-02-15

Each time a picture of Martha Stewart pops up on my Instagram feed, I wonder what her secret is; at 82 years old, her skin looks radiant, plump, and shockingly youthful. Last week, she finally gave me the answers I’d been looking for.

During the season two premier of The Martha Stewart Podcast — an iHeartPodcasts production — the star talked about all things skincare and maintenance with her cosmetic dermatologist, Dr. Dan Belkin. And while they “set the record straight” about what work she has had done — citing Botox, filler, and skin-tightening procedures — I was most interested in what she wears “every single morning” to maintain her glow. 

According to Stewart, Dr. Belkin put her on to Alastin’s tinted, hydrating sunscreen, and she now “loves [it] so much” she won’t “leave the house with it.” “I add to it a little serum, so I thin it out a little tiny bit, and I just put it all over my face,” chest, arms, and hands, she explained, adding that, “It really works, and it looks like you have some makeup on.” 

Alastin HydraTint Pro Mineral Broad Spectrum Sunscreen

Alastin’s HydraTint Pro Mineral Broad Spectrum Sunscreen with SPF 36 serves three purposes: to protect skin from harmful UV rays, provide a boost of all-day hydration, and give skin an even, glowy appearance. “It’s sunscreen and a glowy skin tint in a tube,” said an InStyle senior beauty editor, adding that it has “a skin-perfecting finish.” And while Dr. Belkin noted that the brand and the non-comedogenic product are a favorite among dermatologists, the hydrating, tinted sunscreen is also a staple among shoppers, boasting hundreds of perfect ratings.
