Early Life Of Khary Payton

Payton was born in Augusta, Georgia on May 16th, 1972. He got interested in acting in high school and as a teenager did stand up. After Payton graduated Southern Methodist University, he acted in some stage shows in Dallas, Texas.

Payton decided in high school that he wanted to be an actor, and he spent a lot of time as a theater student at Southern Methodist University and post-college time doing Shakespeare theater productions.

Khary Payton's Career

Khary Payton is best known for his voice role of the DC Comics character Cyborg across various films, cartoons, and video games. He is also known for his live-action performances on General Hospital and The Walking Dead, as well as roles in Emily's Reasons Why Not, Generator Rex, and Ben 10.

Khary Payton's Personal Life

Khary Payton married twice. He first married an actress Linda Braddock in 2001 and divorced in 2009. He then married his second wife, an actress Stacy Reed Payton in 2010, and they have two children together. Stacy has logged guest roles on a variety of TV shows, including Weeds, Private Practice, My Name Is Earl, The West Wing, and Criminal Minds.
