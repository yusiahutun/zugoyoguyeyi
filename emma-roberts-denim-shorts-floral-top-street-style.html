Emma Roberts Wears Denim Short Shorts and Floral Top in L.A.

Publish date: 2024-07-01

Not even the scorching California heat can put a damper on Emma Roberts's style.

The 25-year-old star hit the streets of Los Angeles yesterday wearing the perfect summer look, and we want to copy her whole outfit immediately. To run errands, Roberts donned a breezy plunging long-sleeve floral top paired with high-waist dark denim short shorts that highlighted her lengthy gams. The blonde beauty completed her ensemble with brown suede block heel Louise et Cie sandals, pale yellow Garrett Leight x Clare V. sunnies, a delicate silver necklace, and a tasseled black leather crossbody bag. She left her lengthy strands down in loose waves.

But this isn't the first time the actress has shown off her killer physique. Last month she was spotted rocking a super low-cut striped jumpsuit with platforms heels while out in New York City, and days later the fashionista was snapped taking a swim in a hot pink ruffled bikini while on a quick vacation in Miami.

Roberts is currently in the middle of filming the second season of her hit TV series Scream Queens, which premieres Sept. 20 on Fox.
