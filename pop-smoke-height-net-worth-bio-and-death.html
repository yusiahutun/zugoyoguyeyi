Pop Smoke Early Life 

Pop Smoke was born in Brooklyn, New York, in the United States. Together with his brother, he grew up in Obasi, Canarsie, Brooklyn,

Pop Smoke Career

Pop began his music career in 2018 after visiting a Brooklyn recording studio with fellow rapper Jay Gwaupo.

Before releasing original tracks, he used to produce mixtapes of hit songs by other artists using beats from YouTube.

In April 2019, Pop signed to Universal Music’s Victor Victor Worldwide, and on April 23, 2019, he released his first single, Welcome to the Party, produced by 808Melo, which became a major hit.

Two versions of this song featuring Nicki Minaj and Skepta were later released.

On July 26, 2019, Pop released his debut mixtape, Meet the Woo, Pop released many singles from October to December, including War with Lil Tjay and 100K on a Coupe with Calboy.

He was featured in Travis Scott’s single Gatti, which peaked at number 69 on the US Billboard 100 on December 27, 2019.

On January 16, 2022, Smoke released his single Christopher Walking.

Then, on February 7, 2020, he released his second mixtape, Meet the Woo 2, which featured Quavo, Lil Tjay, A Boogie wit da Hoodie, and Five Foreign.

The mixtape broke several records in the American music industry. It ranked at number seven on the US Billboard 200, giving Smoke his first top-ten hit in us.

In 2020, he released his debut album, Shoot for the Stars, Aim for the Moon, which was also a huge hit. The album’s title track, Make It Rain, featured Rowdy Rebel and other talented musicians.

But Pop became popular mostly because of his aggressive and minimal instrumentation, which he learnt from collaborations with British hip hop musicians and producers.

Not only was he a well-known Rapper Pop was also popular on social media platforms.

His official Instagram account currently has over 3.8 million followers.

His YouTube account has over 6.59 million subscribers, and his videos have received over 3.6 billion views.

Pop Smoke Death

Bashar Barakah famously known as Pop Smoke died when he was only 20. Pop Smoke had starred in the Hollywood picture Boogie before his death.

He was murdered on February 19, 2020 during a tragic home invasion robbery. At 4:30 A.M, five hooded males entered the residence through a second-story balcony, including one wearing a ski mask and carrying a weapon. The attackers threatened to shoot a woman in the head with a gun. According to the woman, the men shot Pop three times after a dispute. 

After the incident, police arrived and found Jackson with several gunshot wounds. After being taken to Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, doctors performed a thoracotomy on the left side of his chest.

A few hours later, the doctors declared him dead. On February 21, the Los Angeles County Department of Medical Examiner-Coroner confirmed that Jackson died of a gunshot wound to the torso.

Rapper Pop Smoke was put to rest in Greenwood Cemetery. 

Sadly, the suspects have not been arrested and have not been recognized so far. It is unsure whether Pop Smoke knew his attackers.

Despite his quick breakthrough in hip-hop, Pop Smoke remains one of the new generation’s most overlooked artists. His number one album shows that if he had been given more time to emerge, the rapper would have become one of the industry’s most successful names.
