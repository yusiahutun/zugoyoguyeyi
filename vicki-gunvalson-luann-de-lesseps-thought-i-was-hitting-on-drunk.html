LuAnn De Lesseps Thought I Was Hitting On Drunk Rey!

Publish date: 2024-06-13

Vicki Gunvalson is always keeping everyone updated on the status of her love tank.

So imagine her surprise when The Real Housewives of New York‘s LuAnn de Lesseps thought the O.C. blond was moving in on her man.

Gunvalson recapped the argument for Andy Cohen on the October 10th episode of Watch What Happens Live.

At the time, the Countess was seeing Rey Setter, the high-brown hot mess she ran into at Dorinda Medley and John Mahdessian’s dry cleaning party.

There, Setter tried to explain to Ramona Singer and Sonja Morgan that he and LuAnn were a thing, but he was a bit overserved so none of it really made any sense.

According to Gunvalson, de Lesseps got territorial with her while they were all out together.

"Luann and I got in a fight that night because she thought that I wanted him," Gunvalson said.

"I’m like, ‘I don’t want him, he’s yours.’ I just met him…I don’t know who he is, but she’s like, ‘Keep your hands off him’…he was all over me…who is he?’"

Those frisky Housewives!

"He was like, ‘I want your number, I think your beautiful,’" Gunvalson added.

"Luann, you can have him. I’m good."

Setter posted a memo to his Instagram account back in May, clarifying that whiel he and de Lesseps did date, they were no longer together.

"I know most of you would like to know, I was seeing Luann for about a year," Setter explained.

"We met at the NBC after party at NO8 in NYC, we had an instant attraction. Soon after we spent our weekends in the Hamptons, and Europe. Shortly after fall fashion week we mutually decided to go our separate ways.

"I wish her all the best. #nyc #rhony #bravo #rey #tbt"

De Lesseps is set to marry businessman Tom D’Agostino on New Year’s Eve in Palm Beach, even though he was caught making out with another woman last season on RHONY.

Gunvalson, meanwhile, is dating retired cop Steve Lodge.
