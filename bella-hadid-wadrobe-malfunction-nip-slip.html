Bella Hadid Has a Nip-Slip at London Fashion Week

Publish date: 2024-04-03

Last night, Bella Hadid stepped out in a perfectly vamped-up, leather-on-leather look: a Misha Collection wrap dress, black over-the-knee boots and a cross choker necklace to add a Buffy-esque gothic touch. The ensemble fit the model well, especially since it matched her newly-dark locks, as she attended LOVE Magazine and Burberry's London Fashion Week party.

But Bella seemed to struggle to keep her plunging neckline together...

Getty Images

By the time she got in the car, the deep-V frock shifted, and she suffered a major nip-slip.

The model hasn't responded to the wardrobe malfunction (the moment was probably so brief, she didn't notice it happened), but Hadid is no stranger to freeing the nipple—on purpose. During couture week, she sported a sheer Dior gown with a barely-there bustier, schooling us all on the see-through top trend.

Getty Images

But if she did want to publicly react to the slip-up, perhaps Chrissy Teigen could offer some inspiration.
