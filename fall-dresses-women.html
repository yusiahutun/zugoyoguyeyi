30 Best Fall Dresses of 2023

Publish date: 2024-06-14

Best Scoop Neck Sweater Dress

Hill House Home The Maude Dress

Best Scoop Neck Sweater Dress

Hill House Home The Maude Dress

Now 30% Off

Credit: Hill House Home

As a semi-professional Hill House dress collector, I think this may be the label's best sweater dress to date. The sweeping scoop neckline and blouson sleeves are pretty and polished, while the wool blend fabrication guarantees and cozy and comfortable day wearing it. I'm envisioning myself wearing it with a long black hair ribbon and Miu Miu flats for a balletic moment, but the minimal design could go in all sorts of aesthetic directions.

Sizes: XXS–XXL

Colors: Gray, black

Best Slouchy Fall Sweater Dress

Altuzarra Kasos Cashmere Dress

Best Slouchy Fall Sweater Dress

Altuzarra Kasos Cashmere Dress

Credit: Moda Operandi

Can a fall sweater dress be … sexy? Altuzarra says yes, with this cashmere midi that sensually drapes off one shoulder. Note that the powder blue is also a fall 2023 trend—but it’s slightly more versatile than the fire-engine red resurgence happening now.

Sizes: XS–L

Colors: Blue

Best Reversible Dress

Diane von Furstenberg Verina Reversible Mesh Dress

Best Reversible Dress

Diane von Furstenberg Verina Reversible Mesh Dress

Now 26% Off

Credit: Diane von Furstenberg

A mesh dress with a ruched skirt, high mockneck, and seasonal print would earn my "Add to Cart" all on their own. But this dress comes with a secret: It completely reverses to a second gorgeous print, so it's essentially two sleek dresses in one. Layer one size with a leather blazer and boots, then try the other with a merino wool sweater and flats.

Sizes: XXS–XL

Colors: Pansy rosewood/autumn berries, Paris floral/tiny bean

Best Belted Sweater Dress

Faherty Jackson Long Sleeve Tie Waist Sweater Dress

Best Belted Sweater Dress

Faherty Jackson Long Sleeve Tie Waist Sweater Dress

Now 20% Off

Credit: Nordstrom

Here's a sweater dress that will still give you a little structure while allowing you to feel bundled up, thanks to its coordinating tie-waist belt. I'm as tempted to try this with my Reformation x Veja sneakers and chunky socks as I am with a sleek knee-high boot.

Sizes: XXS–XXL

Colors: Claret, oatmeal

What reviewers are saying: "This is a fantastic effortless sweater dress. I feel it runs slightly large, as I originally ordered a medium and exchanged for a small."

Best Mixed-Print Fall Dress

Rixo Meg Dress

Best Mixed-Print Fall Dress

Rixo Meg Dress

Credit: Rixo

This Rixo dress is at the top of my personal wish list. I love how the designers inventively combined two quintessential fall prints—the basic black-white polka dot and an exaggerated leopard print—into a single dress with a layered effect.

Sizes: 2–22

Colors: Leopard/dot, startlet floral, poppy/leopard, and more

What reviewers are saying: “Absolutely adore this dress and know I’m going to get so much wear out of it, a forever dress. I sized down - I’m a 12 but was a bit big so kept the size 10.”

Best Fall Runway Dress

Proenza Schouler Nappa Leather Strapless Dress

Best Fall Runway Dress

Proenza Schouler Nappa Leather Strapless Dress

Credit: Proenza Schouler

Behold, the red leather dress that sparked a bona fide color trend and all sorts of social media chatter when it arrived on Proenza Schouler’s runway. It’s the sort of investment that’s hard to resist: The construction and saturated color are unlike any other leather dress on the market.

Sizes: 0–12

Colors: Red

Best Quilted Fall Dress

Loeffler Randall Lottie Dress

Best Quilted Fall Dress

Loeffler Randall Lottie Dress

Credit: Loeffler Randall

Jessie Loeffler Randall said this quilted dress strikes a special balance: “It’s a statement dress that also feels down to earth.”

She added: “It’s got the appearance of being layered and I love that! It’s actually very lightweight and airy. I’ll be wearing it with our tall shaft Gia boots or with some statement velvet platforms.”

Sizes: XS–XL

Colors: Cream floral, blush floral

Best Tiered Off-the-Shoulder Dress

Sea Loren Dress

Best Tiered Off-the-Shoulder Dress

Sea Loren Dress

Credit: Amazon

Has there every been a more romantic fall dress than this Sea piece? Whether in the dominating shade of the season or a moodier black fabric, the dreamily gathered neckline and frothy tiered skirt belongs in your lineup of fall galas, weddings, and other formal occasions.

Sizes: XXS–XL

Colors: Red, Black

Best Punk Fall Plaid Dress

Vivienne Westwood Checked Midi Dress

Best Punk Fall Plaid Dress

Vivienne Westwood Checked Midi Dress

Plaid is as essential to fall as pumpkin lattes and Nora Ephron films. This Vivienne Westwood dress takes the classic seasonal check and makes it daring, sculptural, and a little punk rock—true to the house’s counterculture roots.

Sizes: 0–10

Colors: Checked blue

Gestuz Ruched Round-Neck Midi Dress

Best Wedding Guest Dress

Best Wedding Guest Dress

Credit: Farm Rio

Best Fall Floral Dress

Dôen Bijou Dress

Best Fall Floral Dress

Dôen Bijou Dress

Credit: Dôen

When I wore this dress to work on a recent August morning, I was stopped three times between my apartment and my office by strangers complimenting it. I have to agree it’s one of Dôen’s absolute best. The brand updates this square-neck midi with a smocked bodice each season with new fabrics, and each variation embodies bohemian elegance. (Plus, it’s unbelievably comfortable). I plan to layer a brown leather blazer over the top when fall really kicks in.

Sizes: XXS–XXL

Colors: Navy floral

Tibi Tencel Cotton Shirtdress
