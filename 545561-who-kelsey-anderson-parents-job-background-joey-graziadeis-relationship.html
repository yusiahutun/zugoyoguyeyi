Early life

Kelsey grew up alongside her four siblings. Her sister, Taylor Anderson, attended the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. Her brother Pascal joined the military, following in his father's footsteps.

Jordan, Kelsey's older sister, attended Northwestern State University, and her younger brother Matthew played offensive lineman on his high school football team and for the University of Nebraska and Campbell University.

After graduating from high school, Kelsey attended Saint Leo University in Florida. She earned an associate's degree in general studies and humanities and a bachelor's of business administration in project management and graduated in 2021.
