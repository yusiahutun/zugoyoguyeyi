Girl Scouts to Host Sales at Duffys Sports Grill Locations

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Local Girl Scouts troops will be stationed outside of 23 different Duffy’s Sports Grill locations throughout Palm Beach County, Broward County, Martin County, and St. Lucie County on Friday, February 26, as part of the “Give a Box, Get a Box” Cookies for the Military campaign. For every box of cookies purchased for the military, guests will receive a free box of cookies to take home. This is the ninth year that Duffy’s Sports Grill will be involved with this program with the Girl Scouts and have together donated more than 25,000 boxes for the military. The goal this year is to sell more than 3,000 boxes to send to military personnel.
