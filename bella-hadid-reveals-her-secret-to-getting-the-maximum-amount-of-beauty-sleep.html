Bella Hadid reveals secret to getting enough sleep

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Being a supermodel may seem glamorous, but flying around the world for photo shoots with really early call times would be gruelling, especially when you're trying to force yourself to sleep in completely different time zones. Plus, it's essential that you look bright-eyed to do your job.

Bella Hadid has revealed her trick to getting a good night's sleep, even when on a rigorous Fashion Week schedule. Speaking to Teen Vogue, the model said that she uses meditation music to help her nod off, saying she is "usually out by the fourth song".

"I recently started listening to meditation music, which makes me have a deeper sleep, even if I just have four hours," she said. The one she recommends? 50 Best Meditation Songs Collection.

The 20-year-old also uses face masks both morning and night to keep her skin looking refreshed. "In the morning, I always like to do a sheet mask because it revives me to have something cool on my skin," she said.

Meditation music and masks. Duly noted.
