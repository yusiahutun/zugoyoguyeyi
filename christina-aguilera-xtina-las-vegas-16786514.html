Dirrty! Christina Aguilera, 38, models nipple tassels in racy topless photoshoot

Publish date: 2024-07-10

The Dirrty songstress is gearing up to become a permanent fixture on the Las Vegas strip.

In advance of her onstage debut, Christina Aguilera channelled the spirit of Sin City in a provocative photo shoot for Galore Magazine.

The cover shoot, which was photographed by camera pro Ellen Von Unwerth, shows the starlet in a variety of saucy poses.

Surrounded by casino props and showgirl feathers, the 38-year-old got into the swing of things by flashing a little flesh.

Taken in black and white, the cover shot chosen for the magazine looked straight out of old school Hollywood.

Her hair was styled in screen-siren curls and she'd added a sleek red lip and flicked eye makeup.

By way of accessories, she donned a pearl-encrusted robe and waved a stack of dollar bills in one hand.

Turning up the heat, the singer wore fishnet stockings and went utterly topless - apart from a pair of nipple tassels.

Her nipple tassels took centre stage in most of the shots - some were decorated with miniature diamond-studded machine guns, while others were simply plain black.

Other pics from the shoot show her wearing just a pink feather boa or tiger-print gloves.

The mum-of-two shared the steamy snaps on her Instagram, leading to a bevvy of compliments from her 5.9million followers.

One said: “We are not worthy of these photos...”

A second added: “You’re giving me a heart attack!”

Instagram

And a third admirer wrote: “Oh damn!”

Christina, who goes by her stage name “Xtina”, titled her highly-anticipated show as “the eXperience”.

With a stellar reputation for performance, the Californian beauty is set to make her mark on Las Vegas from May 31 onwards.

She will join a legion of strong female artists to have held a Vegas residency in the past, successfully captivating the crowds night after night with their music.

Legendary pop divas like Britney Spears, Celine Dion and Cher are just a few of the names to have taken the US city by storm.

And, if this cover shoot is anything to go by, Christina Aguilera will fit right in with the iconic bunch.
