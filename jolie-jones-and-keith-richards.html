Jolie Jones appears in the 2012 Rolling Stones documentary "Crossfire Hurricane" as Keith Richards' girlfriend as the Stones were touring Los Angeles, CA in summer 1972. "There's an especially adorable visual of her in what appears to be a yellow bikini, in an expansive hotel room, surrounded by the band and their entourage, standing on her toes, in Keith's arms, and they're kissing each other. Cute. Just in case you were wondering (because you know I was) it's none other than Jolie Jones -- the eldest daughter of legendary producer/musician Quincy Jones. In the late 60s, she was a model -- the first African-American on the cover of Mademoiselle and featured in the pages of Seventeen Magazine." (1queenesther).
