With the influx of reality stars stealing attention from the A-list, Kim Kardashian will be on her toes.

Evelyn showcased an enviable and natural body shape while soaking up the sunshine at Miami Beach, Florida, sporting a revealing two-piece that was more reminiscent of underwear than a bikini.

While Evelyn hasn't quite reached global status, the 36-year-old is known known for her romance with NFL player Chad Ochocinco - as well as a prior 10-year romance with NBA star Antoine Walker.

Writing on her website, Evelyn wrote: 'I’m so happy to announce that I will be making my own custom milkshake at Millions of Milkshakes in West Hollywood to benefit The American Cancer Society!
