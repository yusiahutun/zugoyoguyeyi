Governors Ball Returns To NYC In June

Publish date: 2024-05-27

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — Founders Entertainment’s Governors Ball music festival will make its return to New York this year, taking place at Flushing Meadows Corona Park from June 7-9 for the second year in a row.

For 2024, the festival will feature 3 days and 60 bands, performing across three full stages of music. Headliners announced for this year include Post Malone, The Killers, SZA, Peso Pluma, Rauw Alejandro and 21 Savage.

The lineup also includes scheduled performances from Dominic Fike, Carly Rae Jepsen, Labrinth, Goth Babe, Yung Gravy, Sabrina Carpenter, Sexyy Red, Don Toliver, and more.

Festival organizers also recently revealed the culinary lineup for the event, which will feature offerings from some of New York’s leading eateries.
