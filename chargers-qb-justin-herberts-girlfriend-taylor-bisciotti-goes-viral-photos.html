Chargers QB Justin Herberts Girlfriend Taylor Bisciotti Goes Viral (Photos)

Publish date: 2024-02-17

Los Angeles Chargers quarterback Justin Herbert’s girlfriend, Taylor Bisciotti, went viral this past week.

As Herbert prepares for his fourth season as a pro, expectations are sky high for him and his team. Last year, many expected L.A. to take a major step forward following an unbelievable 2021 campaign from the former Oregon standout.

Instead, in 2022, Herbert wound up throwing for three hundred less yards, 13 fewer touchdowns and recorded a QB rating that was four points lower. To add insult to injury, his Charges got humiliated in the opening round of the playoffs by the Jacksonville Jaguars.

As Herbert aims for redemption in 2023, his relationship with Bisciotti has fallen under the microscope this week. This is unsurprising, given that Herbert often finds himself in the news cycle for female-related issues. Be it him possibly signing a fan’s butt or another fan’s provocative offer for him, it’s one thing after another.

Although Herbert and Bisciotti have never publicly acknowledged a romance, speculation about the pair has run rampant for more than a year. It has been described as “a ‘low-key’ relationship,” but a relationship nevertheless.
