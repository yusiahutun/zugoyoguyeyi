Hot Chelle Rae Signs With ICM Partners

Publish date: 2024-04-07

Los Angeles, CA (CelebrityAccess) — ) – ICM Partners announced it has signed the pop-rock band Hot Chelle Rae for worldwide representation in all areas

Hot Chelle Rae which currently consists of Ryan Follesé (lead vocals, rhythm guitar), Nash Overstreet (lead guitar, backing vocals), and Jamie Follesé (drums), first started performing as Hot Chelle Rae in 2005, but they didn’t’ release their debut LP Lovesick Electric until 2009.

Two years later, the group scored a major hit with their platinum-selling followup “Whatever” that included charting singles “Tonight Tonight” and “I Like It Like That.”

Following a several-year hiatus, Hot Chelle Rae is back in the studio, with plans for a major international tour later this year. The band’s fanbase, which is eagerly anticipating new music, is active on the group’s socials with 2.3 million monthly listeners and approximately 400,000 followers on Spotify, 300,000 YouTube subscribers, 200,000 on band Instagram accounts, and with more than 1 million followers on Facebook.

At ICM, the band will be represented by a team of agents led by Mike Hayes and Scott Mantell. Hot Chelle Rae is managed by Jared Rosenberg and Robert Roig for Rosenberg Management.

“We are truly excited and honored to be working with such a talented and unique group that crosses so many different genres. We cannot wait for what’s to come,” Hayes and Mantell said in a joint statement.
