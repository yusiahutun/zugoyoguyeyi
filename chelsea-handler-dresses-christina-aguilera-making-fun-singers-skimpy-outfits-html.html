Christina Aguilera is known for her revealing outfits, which often sees her sporting a tiny pair of shorts on The Voice.

Talk show host Chelsea Handler is not a fan, and after joking about the star's penchant for wearing underwear style clothing on her show Chelsea Lately several times, the blonde presenter decided to try the look for herself.

Welcoming fellow The Voice judge Blake Shelton in her studio, 37-year-old talk show host donned a typical Christina ensemble in an effort to make the country singer 'feel more at home'.

Getting dirty: Chelsea Handler dresses up as Christina Aguilera to surprise her fellow The Voice coach Blake Shelton to her show

Dressed in a low cut basque and the typical tiny hot pants with fishnet tights, the presenter tried to look more like Christina Aguilera to welcome The Voice coach.

And Shelton was certainly surprised, as he burst into laughter when he walked out on stage and saw Chelsea's get-up.

Shaking his head, the country star first pretended to turn back before laughing and saying: 'Happy Christmas to ME!'

'I wanted to make you feel at home': The TV presenter explains the joke to the country singer, who laughs along

Almost identical: Chelsea makes fun of Christina Aguilera's style choices, which often involve a lack of pants

Explaining her unusual attire, Chelsea said: 'I wanted to dress like Christina to make you more comfortable. I wanted you to feel like you were at your own job.'

In her typical jokey style, the presenter continued: 'I know you had to travel five minutes in a golf cart from your studio to this studio and I know with your drinking schedule how hard that must of been!'

However, Blake wasn't thrown off by the cheeky comments and replied stone faced: 'I didn't hear a word of what you said.'

Warm welcome: The bubbly blonde welcomed Blake Shelton to her show dressed in the revealing costume

Good sense of humour: Blake played along and laughed off the joke as the presenter took a few more jibes at hime

Chelsea wasn't the only one that was dressed up for the interview, as she had her trusty sidekick Chuy wear a bright red fluffy coat in the style of the other famous The Voice judge Cee Lo Green.

This isn't the first time the cheeky presenter has dressed up as the famous singer on her show.

She made comments about Christina's underwear-like outfits before, and when the Dirty hit maker finally appeared on the popular programme herself, she had her revenge.

'I heard all about the s*** Chelsea's been talking about me not being a fan of wearing pants, and proudly,' the singer said.

'So when the asked me to come on I said, "I'll do it if Chelsea does one thing for me ..."', after which the presenter emerged in the costume.
