See Kendall Jenner look Parisian chic in a grey jumper dress

Publish date: 2024-06-15

Kendall Jenner's trip to Paris is delivering one sophisticated look after another.

The model continued her international getaway with yet another chic outing. Jenner was photographed out in the French capital, where she wore a cosy spin on her typical sleek style.

The reality star's ensemble comprised of a grey wool dress by Ann Demeulemeester, featuring long sleeves, an asymmetrical thigh-high slit, and a mock neck with an attached full-length scarf adornment over the right shoulder. She styled the piece with knee-high leather boots by The Row, a black leather tote and oval sunglasses. As for her beauty look, Jenner pulled her hair back in a middle-part ponytail worn with a simple nude lip.

BACKGRID

Last week, Jenner channelled Audrey Hepburn on her stylish Parisian trip. For her vintage-inspired look, the model wore a dress by The Row and paired it with a black headscarf, which she wrapped around her neck and left trailing down the back. She accessorised the minimalistic ensemble with black pointed-toe leather kitten heels, and black elongated sunglasses.

BACKGRID

Jenner is well known for her sophisticated street style but this year she seems to have elevated her wardrobe. Earlier this month, she was seen out in Beverly Hills wearing an off-duty look comprised of a shiny brown trench coat with gold buttons from FWRD, a simple white tee from Leset, a beige maxi skirt with a high-leg slit, and black leather knee-high boots with bent stiletto heels. She also looked chic during a February outing in Milan, where she wore a sleek all-black business look broken up by a cream shearling jacket with ivory fur detailing.
