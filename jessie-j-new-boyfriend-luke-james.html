Jessie J goes public with new boyfriend Luke James

Publish date: 2024-03-05

Jessie J has confirmed that she is dating singer/songwriter Luke James, after weeks of speculation that the pair are more than just friends. Sharing a cute photo of herself and Luke on Instagram, Jessie made her new romance known by captioning the snap: "My first ever official #mcm. My man. My crush. My Monday morning. So it's only right. @wolfjames." The 26-year-old looked to be on cloud nine as she was pictured cuddling up to her boyfriend and smiling sweetly at the camera.

Luke, likewise, echoed her sentiments by posting a selfie on the picture-sharing site with his "babe" Jessie in the background. "Problem? lol | my bro @mrjewelzxavier and my babe@isthatjessiej and I mobbing my city streets. #neworleans | never too much fun! #Love | #LukeJames," Luke, who has written songs for stars such as Britney Spears, Keri Hilson and Justin Bieber, wrote. Jessie has recently revealed that her bisexuality was just a "phase" and that she resented being defined as an artist by her sexual orientation.

Jessie J has previously said she was 'bored' of talking about her sexuality

"I did talk about it, and I was open about it, and I do support being lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender – love who you want," she said. "That's what I'm doing. I don't regret anything I ever said, but I never knew back then that whatever I said became a fact that I couldn't change. "I'm just so bored of it, and that's kind of it – I want to stop talking about it completely now and find myself a husband." Jessie also took to Instagram on Monday to announce some more exciting news. The Bang Bang hitmaker will be back on tour in the UK next year, playing shows in Glasgow, Newcastle, Leeds, Manchester, Birmingham and London. "What a perfect way to start 2015!" Jessie captioned a photo of herself striking a pose.
