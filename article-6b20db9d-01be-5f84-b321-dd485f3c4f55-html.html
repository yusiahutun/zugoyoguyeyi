Jones joins Jerry Ford Real Estate |

Publish date: 2024-03-29

Jones

Donna Jones, a graduate of the Mississippi Realtor Institute, recently joined Jerry Ford Real Estate in Ellisville.

She is a multi-million-dollar producer with a past record of closing numerous real estate deals. She was chosen “Rookie of the Year” by the Laurel Board of Realtors and a Top 5 producer in 2015.

Before becoming a licensed real estate agent, she earned a Baccalaureate of Science degree from the University of Southern Mississippi and worked as a registered nurse for more than 25 years.

She grew up in Laurel and is married to Robin Jones, who has one son, Ridge. During her free time, Jones enjoys fishing, attending sporting events and spending time with her family.

Jones is a member of Kington Assembly of God church, where she supports and participates in various areas of the ministry. She also is an avid animal lover and works hard to support abused and abandoned animals in the community.

She credits her personal Christian values — commitment, compassion and honesty — as the reasons for her real estate success.
