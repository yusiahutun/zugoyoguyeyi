Biography

Malcolm McDowell was born in Horsforth, England on June 13, 1943. The British actor is best known for his starring role as Alex DeLarge in Stanley Kubrick‘s A Clockwork Orange, based on the novel by Anthony Burgess. He has also played roles on Entourage, Star Trek Generations, and Our Friends in the North. In 2012, he received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. He had a recurring role in the Amazon Prime series Mozart In The Jungle, acting alongside Gael García Bernal. His first few jobs involved serving drinks at his parents’ pub and working as a coffee salesman.
