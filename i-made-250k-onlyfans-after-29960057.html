Her GP suggested her issues were due to the heaviness of her breasts and referred her to a surgeon who diagnosed her with the condition.

Gigantomastia is very rare and causes rapid, excessive breast tissue growth, which is why Pamelia went from a J cup bra size to an O cup in just eight months.

There is a chance her breasts could grow back to their current size after the reduction, but she is looking forward to having some of her discomforts eased.

"Overall, I'm glad this happened to me, because I've been able to open people's eyes to gigantomastia, I'm happy that I've had the experience," she said.
