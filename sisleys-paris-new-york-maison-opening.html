Sisley Maison New York City Spa Details, Review, Experience

Publish date: 2024-10-04

Family-owned luxury beauty brand Sisley has been opening salons all over the world to accompany its original Parisian location—including locations in Bordeaux and Lyon, France; Milan, Italy; and Brussels, Belgium. The latest opening brings the Maison Sisley to New York City at 652 Hudson Street. The full-service stand-alone spa and boutique showcases the best of the French brand’s spa treatments, skincare, makeup, fragrance, and hair care products—and even art.

BOOK

Courtesy Sisley Paris

Each of Sisley’s maisons harbors a signature whimsical look that both connects and differentiates it from the others. The New York location includes custom wallpaper and an oversize, hand-painted lampshade designed by Polish artist Elzbieta Radziwill—incidentally, the niece of Isabelle d’Ornano, who founded Sisley in 1976 with her husband, Hubert. In one of the hallways, an Andy Warhol screen print of French writer and filmmaker Jean Cocteau adorns the wall. Every detail of the space and the pieces that fill it were hand chosen by d’Ornano and her daughter Christine to add a decidedly NYC charm.

BOOK

Courtesy Sisley Paris

What really sets this location apart is the addition of Hair Rituel by Sisley’s Le Grand Soin for Hair. This new, exclusive 60- or 90-minute experience brings together the art of massage (inspired by the shiatsu method) and hair care from the brand’s Hair Rituel line of products. The tailored treatments are meant to both nourish the hair and stimulate the scalp, while providing a relaxing and ultra-sensory experience. After your treatment, a hairstyling service and blow-dry are also provided.

BOOK

Courtesy Sisley Paris

Other services include 30-, 60-, and 90-minute face or body treatments to tackle areas like moisture, cleansing, and sculpting. For serious skin TLC, there’s the Sisleÿa L’Integral La Cure anti-aging treatment or the Supremÿa anti-aging treatment, both of which harness the brand’s most luxurious skincare offerings to lift and smooth. And before you head back out into the world, the maison even has a dedicated makeup area to provide touch-ups and reapplications. A full spa list can be found here.
